History of the Emirate of Ajman

One of the oldest and iconic states in the UAE, Ajman is known for its popular mangrove trees and a larger than life corniche to it. The Emirate is situated along the Persian gulf and enjoys one of the most popular coastal lines to it. In terms of its area, the Emirate is not a big one but in the previous decade, Ajman has reconstructed and renovated so many of its natural locations and added some more developments to the region which has certainly proved fruitful in lifting the overall impression of the Emirate and has also helped in increased movement and tourism into the state.

Why Ajman?

When it comes to Why choose Ajman as a place to reside in? As a neighborhood to make your children grow up in? Why choose Ajman as a place to live and work in?, all these questions can be answered in the flicker of a second just at the mention of the location Ajman enjoys. Ajman is located at a 15 minute drive from Sharjah and hardly that of a 30 min drive from Dubai. Well, the calculations have been taken keeping in mind the traffic flux. If you’re fortunate enough to not get any traffic, the whole Emirate is even nearer than most of the vicinities in Dubai are to each other. Ajman most certainly enjoys an accessible and close proximity to the larger Emirates and that’s the very reason that some of the people working in Dubai and Sharjah are also residing in Ajman because the distance doesn’t count as a barrier to them. Secondly a fact very well known to people living in the UAE, housing and lifestyle in Ajman is extremely affordable as compared to the other Emirates. Within the same amount or even lesser, one can get 2x bigger and spacious accommodations and houses which is very suitable for families as they require larger living spaces. Well, one of most frowned upon factors i.e. money has been taken care of.

Major Recreational Attractions Of The Emirate

Ajman, despite being an absolutely peaceful and affordable Emirate to live in, is also a major attraction and recreation hub featuring a number of several different locations for the residents to relish. e.g. the ancient and antique Ajman Museum, the larger than life Ajman Dhow-Building Yard, the popular and beautiful Ajman Beaches and many other commercial touchpoints in the city.

Abu Dhabi is not only an ideal fit for residential reasons but also for a plethora of recreational landmarks it homes. Major destinations like Ferrari World, Yas Water World and Yas Island, the Sheik Zayed Grand Mosque, and Emirates Park Zoo etc. make Abu Dhabi all the more interesting and intriguing as a place to live in and take your children too.
