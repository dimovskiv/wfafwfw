Alandalus Townhouses by Jumeirah Golf Estates Developer

Alandalus Townhouses by Jumeirah Golf Estates developer is a selection of premium residences located in an enclave of the Jumeirah Golf Estates master development along Mohammed bin Zayed Road. The 95 townhouses of this project boast of being an “affordable luxury” option for clients who are looking for great deals within the prestigious golf community. The freehold residences are styled with Spanish-Arabic designs and finishes making the homes exude a classic and sophisticated feel. Alandalus Townhouses are available in 2 and 3 bedroom units which will be ready by the end of 2018. The project also has a retail center and hotel component.

Premium Amenities

Alandalus Townhouses features a clubhouse that hosts a wealth of communal amenities that the residents can enjoy. Nestled within lush green fairways, the clubhouse hosts a swimming pool, state-of-the-art gym and a kids’ play area. You can also choose from a number of restaurants within the area for your dining pleasure. The community of Alandalus Townhouses features dedicated parking spaces both for its residents and guests. Maintain a fit and healthy lifestyle with the jogging and cycling tracks that blend perfectly with the neighborhood’s natural landscaping. With the two international golf courses easily accessible within the area, the place is well-suited for gold enthusiasts and hobbyists, and even those who are interested in this well-known sport.

Convenient Location

Nestled in the peaceful, green community of Jumeirah Golf Estates, residents can enjoy the beauty of nature and the feeling of living in a quiet hill town. Aside from golf course views, the neighborhood is home to several wildlife species and 400 types of birds, as its lush foliage of 4,000 trees complemented by its 15 magnificent lakes. The area is regarded as one of the greenest residential environments in UAE. Also accessible within the area ar 5 Andalusian heritage sites.
