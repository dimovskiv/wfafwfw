Spanish Tower – Canal Residence West by Dubai Sports City

Canal Residence West – Spanish Tower by Dubai Sports City (DSC) is one of the five towers of the residential project along the Dubai Sports City development. The project’s first three buildings, Mediterranean, European and Venetian towers, have already been completed. Only the Canal Residence West – Spanish Tower and its Arabian Tower are still under construction. This Spanish Tower features 12 storeys with 193 units in total, comprised of 78 studios, 73 one-bedroom units, 38 two-bedroom units and 4 three-bedroom units. The classic residences will be featuring views of the golf course, canal, and Sports Village.

Premium Amenities

The tower exudes classic Mediterranean architecture designs, taking inspiration from the architectural finishes of the southern coast of Spain overlooking the Mediterranean Sea. The Canal Residence West – Spanish Tower showcases vibrant and earthy tones, with a clean design that remind you of the warm winds of coastline winds. The building was designed to make sure that there is beauty in simplicity, taking into consideration even every angle of the building. As you step out from your doorstep, you will be greeted by a collection of restaurants, cafes, supermarkets, pharmacies, optical shops, salons and fashion and electronic retailers within the community.

Convenient Location

Dubai Sports City is the convenient location of Canal Residence West – Spanish Tower. The popular residential community is known for being a sports venue, consisting of mid-rise apartment buildings, villas and sports facilities including an international golf course and a cricket stadium. The premier neighborhood also boasts itself as a city accessible to international schools, world-class medical facilities, hotels, leisure destinations, and even lifestyle country clubs. The area will be featuring a sports-themed retail mall with a leasing capacity of approximately 1.2 million square feet and will be complemented by a multitude of shops, markets, retail plazas, and boutiques.
