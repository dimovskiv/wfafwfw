Green Valley Tower by Green Valley Group Real Estate

Green Valley Tower by Green Valley Group Real Estate is a premier 23-storey residential project located in Jumeirah Village Circle. These high-end apartments boast of stunning views of The Els Golf Club and Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Road. The residences by Green Valley Tower are available in the studio, one, two and three-bedroom units.

Premium Amenities

Access a selection of world-class amenities in Green Valley Tower. The residential building is equipped with 4 basement parking floors and a concierge for every resident’s convenience. Leisure time is can easily be accessed within the building with its swimming pools, gymnasium, kids’ play areas, and indoor sports courts. You can also enjoy an active lifestyle as the community has dedicated jogging tracks.

Vibrant Location

The stunning location of Jumeirah Village Circle provides the perfect environment for Green Valley Tower residences. Specifically nestled on Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Road facing Dubai Sports City, the homes are at the forefront of key destinations such as Al Khail Avenue Mall, Me’aisem City Centre, Circle Mall, and The Els Golf Club. The tower is also just 15 minutes away from Downtown Dubai where you can find Burj Khalifa and Dubai Mall, and only 10 minutes away from Dubai Marina.
