District One Mansions by District One

District One Mansions is a premium selection of 18 luxury mansions located in the opulent community of Mohammed Bin Rashid (MBR) City District. The residences are available in seven and eight-bedroom mansions in different types like traditional Arabesque and modern European designs of the residences are made of meticulously chosen materials which also complement the beauty of its surroundings.

Contemporary & Modern Mediterranean

Inspired with a combination of Mediterranean and European finishes, the contemporary mansions are the perfect homes for those who are looking for warm living spaces that never goes out of style. Enjoy the cool breeze and the sound of soothing water elements right at the comfort of your home, creating that feeling of relaxation at the Mediterranean shores.

Modern Arabic

Experience the Arabian heritage and culture with the homes’ intimate courtyards and traditional joint living areas. The residences are perfect for hosting gala dinner and late night gatherings with a basement cinema. If you wish to have private time, there is an elegant sauna for you to experience the ultimate relaxation.

Premium Amenities

District One Mansions by Meydan Sobha offer top-of-the-line amenities that ensure the ultimate comfort in every aspect. The open plan living spaces showcase full views of the Dubai skyline. Each bedroom features walk-in closets, en-suite bathrooms, and terraces. a staff kitchen, a presentation kitchen, and a maid’s room. The upper floor provides a generous balcony and large rooms, including master’s bedroom and a study. The residences are nestled on a large plot which has four garages and spacious lush gardens.

Stunning Location

District One Mansions is perfectly located in the marvelous community of Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum City. The residences are at the forefront of the finest leisure facilities including the Meydan Racecourse and Grandstand, The Track, Meydan Golf, and a 9-hole floodlit golf course. Soon to rise within the prime area is the Meydan One Mall for convenient shopping and leisure. Five-star luxury Meydan Hotel is also just a few blocks away from home as well as other leisure and entertainment destinations.
