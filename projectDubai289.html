Nad Al Sheba Gardens Plot by Meraas Holding

Nad Al Sheba Gardens is a land development located in Nad Al Sheba 1 near Dubai Al Ain Road. This land for sale in Dubai is located 8 kilometers away from Dubai Mall.

Nad Al Sheba Gardens are available in two zonings: Building and Residential Villa Plots. The permitted height for buildings is G+6 and G+1 for villas.

The plots are for freehold ownership and is available for a two-year payment plan.

Price for Nad Al Sheba Gardens Plot is confidential and will be revealed to interested buyers only.
