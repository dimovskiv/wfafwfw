Redwood Avenue Villas by Jumeirah Golf Estates

Redwood Avenue Villas is a selection of luxury villas located in Jumeirah Golf Estates development. The residences are available in five and six bedroom arrangements with views of the world-class Fire golf course.  The premium villas are also just a few blocks away from its high-end clubhouse. Redwood Avenue Villas surrounding plots are designed by expert landscape architects ensuring the finest surroundings.

Premium Amenities

Redwood Avenue  Villas are designed to provide a range of premium amenities all within reach in the community. The villas provide state-of-the-art audio-visual installations are designed by Bang and Olufsen which exudes gracious living. The kitchens are workmanship of the Poggenpohl brand furnished with Miele appliances and interiors by B&B Italia which fulfills all the requirements of modern-style living. Equipped with a state-of-the-art lighting and access control system, you can easily monitor your home through its different network cameras, turn lights and the A/C on and off which intelligent switches, touch screen, and remote controls or the internet. Redwood Avenue villas are also nestled in individually designed plots with access to landscaped public areas, parks and nature strips that allow you to savor the beautiful outdoors. The landscapes within the community feature natural stone and water features inspired by Mediterranean themes with rock gardens that add a different touch to the design.

Convenient Location

Nestled in the lush master development of Jumeirah Golf Estates, Redwood Avenue Villas are at the forefront of rich wildlife and diversity, being home to about 400 bird types. Within the area are 5 Andalusian heritage sites that connect you to history and culture, and 15 magnificent lakes that creates a refreshing ambience of hill town living. Jumeirah Golf Estates is regarded as one of the greenest neighborhoods in all of UAE, which makes your investment in Red Avenue Villas worth every value.
