The Signature Villas by Green Group

The Signature by Green Group is the developer’s most recent project in Jumeirah Golf Estates. As its name implies, the residential development is in all its true essence a custom made signature artwork of some of the finest maestros, architects and designers globally. Comprising of exquisitely designed and adorned 5BR villas, the houses are all well-ventilated and spaced out in order to ensure the flow of air and a healthy lifestyle for the residents. Being a Green Group’s project, special consideration and profound importance has been given to a facilitated yet sustainable lifestyle within the villas minimizing the usage of artificial sources of energy and lifestyle and promoting a natural eco-friendly one. All the houses are designed following modern Italian craftsmanship with spacious built-in wardrobes, closet spaces and cabinets within kitchens as well as bathrooms to ensure the maximum usage of space. It would not be wrong to say that the houses are all engulfed in luscious green foliages on all sides providing the ultimate sense of being alive and breathing to the residents at all times.

The Signature Villas – Bedroom

Premium Amenities

There are an umpteen number of amenities and state-of-the-art facilities surrounding this beautiful residential development in Jumeirah Golf Estates. Be it the extravagant dedicated swimming pools for men and women, spacious Jacuzzi’s within the villas, sun deck and BBQ areas or gated and secured kids’ play areas, The Signature has got you covered. In addition to all these, the presence of sky terraces, bicycle tracks, gymnasiums, jogging and walking tracks make it all the more favorable for people to reside within this avant-garde of development.

Convenient Location

Due to its location in such a central vicinity as that of Jumeirah Golf Estates, The Signature enjoys close proximity to all the significant key locations within its surroundings. Jumeirah Golf Estates entails a plethora of commotion opportunities for residents living within the vicinity be it metro stations, bus or cab stands etc.
