Acacia Villas Phase 4 by Sharjah Holding

Acacia Villas Phase 4 by Sharjah Holding is the fourth phase of the residential project in the Al Zahia development. The special residences are within the Al Lilac Phase 4 development and are limited in number. The location of the homes is connected to the University Road which allows residents to easily get to the different parts of the emirate. Acacia Villas will be completed by 2020 and are available in 6-bedroom signature villas.

Ready for viewing the actual land space for the exquisite Phase 4 villas before buying them instead of just imagining the space through maps and virtual diagrams.

Premium Amenities

The signature 6-bedroom units of Acacia Villas feature the most elegant living spaces that ensure your privacy and entertainment needs. The homes living room, dining room and majlis area are designed around a double volume entrance hall. This is directly connected to the backyard, with enhanced indoor/outdoor living experience. The residents provide large bedrooms and family lounge spread across the ground and first floors, equipped walk-in wardrobes perfect for the family. The developer now allows its residents to choose between 4 different finishings for their home of choice.

Residents of Acacia Villas Phase 4 can easily access different themed community parks within the development including a water activities park where families and friends can enjoy outdoor activities. The area has a multi-purpose area where residents can play sports to support your active lifestyle. With schools, community centers, and hospitals all accessible within the area, residents are assured of the ultimate convenience.

Convenient Location

Acacia Villas Phase 4 is situated in the Al Zahia development in Sharjah. The premier residential area offers the finest choice of villas, townhouses, and apartments that are all designed of the finest standards. With convenient access to theme parks, multipurpose areas for relaxation and sports and open areas for outdoor activities, you can enjoy the best living experience in the emirate. The strategic location of Acacia Villas Phase 4 is located next to Al Zahia City Center, a shopping center that has similar features with the Mirdif City Center.
