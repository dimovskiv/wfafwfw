The Square by Tiger Group

The Square is a selection of premier residences by Tiger Group located along Al Khail Road in Dubai. The 37-storey furnished apartments feature inspiring designs and well-decorated interiors. The Square is a mixed-use building that hosts its wealth of amenities on the 1st floor, offices on the 2nd and 3rd floor and furnished apartments on the 4th and 37th floor.

The Square Apartments – Bedroom

Premium Amenities

The apartments in The Square feature a choice of studios and one-bedroom apartments that offer astonishing living spaces perfect for a single bachelor or newly-married couple. Step inside the residences and be greeted by an elegant lobby with a reception desk. Enjoy premium amenities such as a fully-equipped gymnasium, kids area, ample parking space, and two swimming pools. The premises are also ensured safe with its 24/7 security system.

The Square Apartments – Swimming Pool

Convenient Location

The Square offers a convenient location with heightened connectivity, being just a few minutes away from major locations like the Dubai International Airport and Al Maktoum International Airport. Enjoy shopping, leisure, and entertainment just a few blocks away from home as it is near the Mall of the Emirates, Downtown Dubai, Global Village and Dubai Miracle Garden.
