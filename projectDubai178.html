Andalusia Residences by Falconcity of Wonders

Andalusia Residences i.e.  Villas are a set of semi-detached villas in the beautiful mega-development of Falconcity of Wonders. As the name rightly implies, Falconcity- the City of Wonders  is actually in truest sense of the word a wondrous city with a plethora of mind-boggling establishments recently introduced within this city. Andalusia Villas and Townhouses is one such residential community. Comprising of 3BR & 4BR villas with immaculate furnishing and extravagantly spacious interiors, this would be a dream come true for anyone looking for a perfect dream home. The villas all come in semi-detached floor plans with their dedicated attached restrooms, guest bedrooms, maid and driver rooms etc. All the Andalusia Villas are designed thematically using serene counter tones to create a tranquilizing and comfortable internal atmosphere inside the houses.

Premium Amenities

Andalusia Villas at Falcon City is a vibrant neighborhood with plenty of recreational and entertainment opportunities for the residents to choose from. With lush green parks in the surroundings, the whole community bears a feel-good essence to it. The residential community comprises of dedicated children’s play area, surrounding marts, town’s best diners and restaurants to eat from, a beautiful mosque and a lively community club for the residents to relish their free time at. Health and wellness facilities like swimming pools, a gym, a basketball court, tennis court and a dedicated jogging track further endorse the very manifesto and ideology of this community i.e. sustainable and comforted living.

Convenient Location

Andalusia Residences by Falcon City is located in Dubailand along Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road with easy access to city’s main highways making travel easier and time efficient for the residents. The beautiful residential development also enjoys close proximity to Dubai International Airport and is accessible to major commotion links i.e. metro stations, cab stands, and bus stops.
