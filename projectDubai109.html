Majan Plot by Dubai Holding

Majan, a modern, diverse mixed-use development, conveniently located on Emirates Road along the largest cycling track. The communities and neighborhoods in Majan are interlinked with large open spaces including axial parks, mid-block connective spaces, and pocket arks, within local urban communities. Spread over 16.5 million square-feet,  Majan is a mega-project that takes care of the needs of its visitors and residents

Being a freehold property, Majan comprises of commercial, residential, retail and leisure sectors. Each section is segregated with precision to highlight the prominence of the area. Majan follows the vision of providing exceptional real estate amenities without compromising on quality. Keeping health and safety policies, Majan properties aim to be one of the best metropolitan centers in Dubai.
