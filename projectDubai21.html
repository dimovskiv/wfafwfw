Aykon Heights By Damac Properties

Thoughtfully designed and immaculately planned, AYKON Heights offers stunning views from every perspective; from Sheikh Zayed Road to the Arabian Gulf; from Dubai Canal to the very heart of Dubai; from Safa Park to Dubai Marina. The dynamic outdoor concept, City Walk is situated just moments away along Al Wasl Road and the Safa Park junction while Boxpark is a striking addition to Jumeirah, where is the convergence of ‘urban chic and contemporary sophistication’.

Boasting an iconic location

AYKON Heights is one of the most iconic settings situated along Sheikh Zayed Road, the most important vehicular artery of Dubai that connects Dubai with Abu Dhabi. The tower overlooks the new Dubai Canal network. It also forms the gateway to the glamorous Burj area.

Getting to AYKON Heights by your car is a pleasant experience through Sheikh Zayed Road. But a more fascinating way to commute is using the canal, from Dubai Marina to Dubai Creek, taking in Jadaf, Business Bay, and Festival City.
