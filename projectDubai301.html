Bvlgari Resort & Residences by Meraas Holding

Bvlgari Resort and Residences is a selection of mansions and apartments set in the amazing location of Jumeirah Bay. The homes are meticulously designed to create efficient living spaces seen in every detail of the home. The 173 apartments and 15 mansions in Bvlgari Resort and Residences all boast of magnificent views, ranging from one to three bedrooms, four bedroom penthouses and three to six bedroom mansions. With a sleek design and well-appointed features, residents are assured of upscale and luxurious living. The homes in Bvlgari Resort and Residences are available in 1 to 3 bedroom apartments, 4 bedroom penthouses, as well as 3, 4 and 5-bedroom mansions.

Premium Amenities

Bvlgari Resort and Residences are designed with spacious interiors that all inspire to create a warm and elegant ambience within the home. Its wooden terraces that stream along its facade are connected with its large glazed sliding doors. The homes are designed with unique architectural designs that seamlessly integrate with the island’s physical elements. Limestone is also used liberally in various parts of the home to add to the sophisticated feel inside the home. With its large glazed windows, you can enjoy the mesmerizing landscape outside. Access private landscaped gardens, swimming pools, and fully-equipped gymnasiums within the building area. You can also enjoy a host of dining and sporting options onsite, as well as the unparalleled services that uphold the standard of the Bvlgari Resort.

Convenient Location

Bvlgari Resort and Residences is located in the magnificent Jumeirah Bay island development. Spanning six million square feet, the mixed-use island development by Meraas is shaped like a giant seahorse riding the arcs of sand and rock. The premier area can be easily accessed off the coast of Jumeirah Beach Road, as the island can be directly accessed via a 300-meter bridge. The island is seen to be the next dream address of those who desire to own private luxury home on the island and enjoy the finest vistas of the sea and the Dubai skyline. Residents of Bvlgari Resort and Residences can avail luxury hotel facilities, including the signature Bvlgari Ristorante and Spa and the Bvlgari Marina and Yacht Club which is the first of its kind in the world.
