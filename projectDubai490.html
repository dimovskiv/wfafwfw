Cayan Cantara Hotel Apartments & Residences by Cayan Group

Cayan Cantara is a collection of luxurious hotel apartments (Arjaan) and branded residences (The Residences) by the distinguished Rotana brand located in Dubai. This state-of-the-art serviced apartment tower in Dubai Science Park offers fully-fitted and fully-furnished studios as well as 1, 2 and 3-bedroom units. With Rotana’s high-end integrated residential environment and entertainment all combined together in one masterpiece, residents are sure to embrace ultimate luxury and elegance.

Enjoy a new dimension of luxury with Cayan Cantara’s variety of carefully designed residential and hotel apartments built according to the most sophisticated international standards in architecture and interior design. The premium apartments also provide direct access to Umm Suqeim Road & a 5-minute distance from the Mall Of Emirates.
