Wasl Gate by Wasl Properties

Wasl Gate is one of the most highly anticipated and foresighted developmental projects by the renowned Wasl Properties in the region. Wasl Gate- literally being designed and marketed as the ‘Gate’ to everything residential, commercial, retail, as well as recreational, is a mixed-use freehold development in the heart of Jebel Ali District along the illustrious Sheikh Zayed Road in Dubai. The whole development has been designed keeping in mind the true spirit of community hood. Wasl Gate is expected to be one of the highly developed and self-sustained developments in the region offering commercial as well as residential plots to the inhabitants. Wasl Gate offers studio, 1BR, 2BR, and 3BR apartments through several sub-projects being built inside it. The houses within the community are all designed and adorned with state-of-the-art furnishing facilities and infrastructure serving majorly as the epitome of poise and suave hand-crafted by some of the best in the bizz.

Premium Amenities

As the whole notion behind the development follows the very basic principle of having access to literally everything at the doorstep, Wasl Gate features premium up-scale amenities for the residents. Ranging from the dedicated Festival Plaza Mall by Al Futtaim Group in the vicinity to possessing ultimate recreation and leisure opportunities like diners, restaurants, Fitness centers, spas and sports grounds within the development, Wasl Gate has it all covered for the people residing within this new-age urban development. One advantage that this development enjoys is its close access to Dubai Festival City which engulfs all the retail, leisure as well as recreational offers within it.

Convenient Location

Wasl Gate definitely enjoys a very central and popular location in terms of the development’s proximity to some major landmarks of the city like Petrol Gas stations, nearby Malls, Healthcare Centers as well to schools and colleges. The project has been designed to serve as a focused vicinity on Jebel Ali Road bearing immediate access to Al Maktoum International Airport and the eagerly anticipated Expo 2020.
