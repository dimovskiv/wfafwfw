Hartland Estates Villas by Sobha Group

Hartland Estates Villas is a mixed-use freehold development located in a prime area of Mohammed Bin Rashid City, which is 3 kilometres away from Dubai’s iconic Burj Khalifa. The development is designed as a complete community and features lifestyle amenities such as freehold villas. The development of Hartland Estates Villas is sprawled over 8 million square feet, where 40% is dedicated to green living. The green spaces feature cycling and walking tracks ideal for active families and community living.

Hartland Estates Villas will comprise of 282 standalone homes with 18-floor plan options enabling homeowners to personalize their contemporary living space. Villa sizes range from 6,259 square feet to 19,432 square feet, spanning across 4, 5 and 6 bedrooms. Each villa will feature contemporary designs with panoramic windows offering breathtaking views of the pool and gardens. Hartland Estates Villas also come with a designated driver’s room and maid’s room.
