DAMAC Residenze by Damac Properties

DAMAC Residenze Luxury Apartments by DAMAC Properties is a selection of luxury apartments with carefully crafted designs by renowned Italian fashion house Fendi Casa. These premier homes are located in the heart of Dubai Marina, one of the most coveted locations in the city. Enjoy views of the Palm Jumeirah from your balcony as well as stunning vistas of the surrounding neighborhood. The residences are available in 1, 2 and 3-bedroom units as well as 5-bedroom penthouses.

Premium Amenities

The levels 43 to 84 of DAMAC Residenze Luxury Apartments will be featuring the luxury apartments fitted out with furnishings inspired by the Fendi Casa brand. The residences’ stylish interiors and comfortable living spaces, these luxury residences perfectly match the vibrant atmosphere of Dubai Marina. Sleep soundly at night without fear and worry with the tower’s advanced security access control, central monitoring and intelligent control of building service. Among the other premium, amenities are ample parking on multiple levels, dedicated lobby for its residences, state-of-the-art gymnasium, as well as separate steam rooms, saunas and jacuzzis for men and women. Kids can also enjoy with a dedicated playroom within the building. Enjoy parties with family and friends in the tower’s multipurpose hall and barbecue area for outdoor quality time over good food.
