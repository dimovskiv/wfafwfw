Dreamz by Danube Properties

Dreamz is a premier project by Danube Properties located in the vibrant community of Al Furjan. These affordable luxury townhouses provide comfortable living spaces for the family, combining the right balance for privacy, safety, and security. Dreamz by Danube is situated over 700,000 square feet of land which is set to be built with state-of-the-art infrastructure facility. The magnificent residences bring you a collection of 171 luxury duplex townhouses with a choice of 3 & 4 bedroom units.

Each house in Dreamz is uniquely designed ensuring comfort and convenience in mind. With wide living spaces for the family combined with amazing features, Dreamz exuberates and compliments the lifestyle for today’s urban family.
