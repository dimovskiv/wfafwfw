Emerald Palace Kempinski Hotel by Emerald Palace Group

Emerald Palace Kempinski Hotel by Emerald Palace Group is a resort hotel project offering a selection of luxury suites, appointed rooms, and villas in the magnificent Palm Jumeirah development. The project is situated over 45,20 square meters of land surrounded by lush tropical gardens. The premier hotel will be operated under the Kempinski brand, will showcase outstanding European-inspired exteriors and Palladian domes which turn into a stunning emerald green color at night. Emerald Palace Kempinski Hotel will also feature stunning views of the sea or the Palm Lagoon.

Premium Amenities

The design of the Emerald Palace Kempinski Hotel exudes sheer elegance with its regal interiors. The designs which are influenced by 18th century continental Europe are by renowned consultant Leo International. The consultancy firm seeks to reflect the era of Louis XV combined with contemporary finishes, which appeals to different guests.

Emerald Palace features crystal chandeliers, complemented by marble, gold, and silver leaf plated wood and superb drapery. The carefully-chosen color schemes easily spell royalty, as the wide spaces are reminiscent of a palatial lifestyle. The classic and elegant design of the hotel also matches the adjacent hotel project by the same developer, Kempinski Residences & Hotel Apartments Palm Jumeirah. Access premium amenities such as temperature-controlled swimming pools, a gym, conference rooms, ballroom among others, all within the hotel tower.

Stunning Location

The stunning location of Palm Jumeirah is the perfect site for Emerald Palace Kempinski Hotel. Enjoy an amazing selection of dining and entertainment attractions including international restaurants, boutique malls, and cinema within the community. Guests will never run out of activities in the area, as different water sports like scuba diving, snorkeling, sailing, surfing, yacht riding, and fishing can be enjoyed in the Gulf. For fitness enthusiasts, get involved in yoga, Pilates and ball games within the community. Kids can also engage in various activities that will surely make them enjoy. The hotel also hosts first-class business and conference facilities, making it an ideal place not only for tourists or leisure travellers, but also to business travelers and corporate people, as well.
