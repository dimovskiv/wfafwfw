22 Carat Penthouse by Forum Group

The 22 Carat Penthouse is a one-of-a-kind phase of the XXII Carat Club project by Forum Group in Palm Jumeirah. This luxury penthouse is regarded as one of the biggest and most elegant penthouses within the master development. Comprising of commodious 8BR Penthouses, it showcases amazing views of the new Blue Waters Island as well as Dubai Eye Ferris Wheel from the third floor. All the 8BR penthouses are designed to perfection by some of the best interior designers in the business. The spacious bedrooms, walk-in closets, kitchen, washrooms, and living areas all follow the same subtle color schemes to bring in the tranquilizing effect of being home.  The first floor is divided into different sections: living quarters on the left, two bedrooms, office, maid’s room, dining area, and the lift lobby. The second floor, on the other hand, features 5 ensuite bedrooms, walk-in closets, and a seating area where they can unwind and relax.

22 Carat Club Villas – Private Beach Access

The roof deck provides a massive terrace with a capacity of 16 persons, as well as a second terrace with totally different view. The penthouse in 22 Carat is strategically positioned several meters off the sea level where you can bask at the sunset on the sea, situated next to the One and Only Resort. The penthouses comprise of two private elevators, a private lobby and a roof deck that boasts of the iconic view of the Dubai Eye and the Marina skyline.

Premium Amenities

The 22 Carat Penthouse in Palm Jumeirah is designed with an amazing layout and equipped with the finest amenities. The first floor of the penthouse features a grand living area with a seating capacity that can accommodate more than 16 people at one time. One can feel absolute royalty and elegance with its grand staircase which leads to astonishing views of the Palm Jumeirah development and the sea. The most private views of the entire palm. The residences are designed with top-of-the-line materials including professional-grade kitchens, custom cabinetry, and lighting facilities designed by master French glass cutters. Residents can also access hotel-like services in its 24/7 concierge service. In addition to all these classic amenities within the residential space itself, the penthouses also have direct access to some fine diners, restaurants and recreational outlets in the vicinity. Apart from these, access the best facilities and amenities in Palm Jumeirah beachfront bars, spa, swimming pools, beach clubs and even a selection of gourmet dining options allowing you to enjoy a complete island lifestyle experience.

Convenient Location

22 Carat Penthouse is situated in the astonishing Palm Jumeirah development, regarded as one of the best residential developments in Dubai.  Popular five-star hotels are surrounding including The Palm Resort, the Atlantis Hotel, and the Jumeirah Zabeel Saray Hotel.  Due to its central location, the residential villa community is accessible by all means of commotion be it nearby metro stations, cab stands, or bus stops.
