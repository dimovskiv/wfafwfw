Belgravia Heights I by Ellington Properties

Belgravia Heights I by Ellington Properties is a choice of premium apartments located in Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC). The luxury residential project is designed with bespoke interiors and elegant exteriors all following Ellington Group’s commitment to create inspiring living spaces. The premier residential building has a vertical facade which emphasizes the corners to create an impression of hierarchy. Belgravia Heights I features 155 apartments comprised of studio, one and two bedroom residences.

Belgravia Heights I – Bedroom

Premium Amenities

Belgravia Heights I welcomes you to a warm-themed home, with a sophisticated color palette composed mainly of whites and warm grey schemes. The residences provide stunning landscaped gardens with dedicated barbeque areas where family and friends can gather. The residences also feature a designed swimming pool for a refreshing swim. There is also a roof terrace and outdoor lounge areas with wide open spaces to make you enjoy the fresh air. Keep your fitness goals in its state-of-the-art gymnasium and health club. Both kids and adults will enjoy its wealth of leisure facilities that all adhere to Ellington Properties’ quality standards.

Belgravia Heights I – Swimming Pool

Convenient Location

Belgravia Heights I is located in the magnificent community of Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC), a community that features a mixture of Mediterranean villas and elegant townhouses. The premier development offers the best community living experience with several facilities now being put up in the area. Several Nakheel developments are seen to rise in the Jumeirah Village area including Al Khail Avenue at Jumeirah Village Triangle and The Circle Mall in Jumeirah Village Circle.
