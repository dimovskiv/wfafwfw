Al Warsan Industrial Plot by Meraas Holding

Al Warsan Industrial Plot by Meraas is a freehold building plot located on Ras Al Khor Road. The land is situated opposite the International City and close to Dragon Mall.

The permitted height for Al Warsan Industrial Plot is G+M for warehouse and refrigerated warehouses, G+5 for labor camp and G+1 for commercial building.

The payment plan for Al Warsan Industrial Plot is 18 months and there is no time limit for the construction of the building. There is also no service charge and 2% of the DLD fee is paid by the developer.
