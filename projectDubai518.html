Royal Bay by Azizi Developments

Royal Bay is a 9-storey luxury serviced apartment project developed by Azizi Developments. It is a beautiful collection of fully-serviced residential apartments situated next to the Rixos Resort on the Crescent of Palm Jumeirah. Royal Bay offers home buyers a unique chance to secure a home in one of the few remaining developments of the so-called Eighth Wonder of the World. The premier residences are cleverly angled at 45-degrees with spectacular views of the city’s skyline, the Atlantis Hotel, and the Arabian Sea.

Royal Bay is designed to maximize light and provide stunning views from every room. The magnificent apartments of Royal Bay enjoy the unique privilege of having direct access to a private beach.
