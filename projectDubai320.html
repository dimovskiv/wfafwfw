Q Line Apartments by Al Mazaya Real Estate

Q-Line Apartments are high-end yet affordable residences located in the Liwan community of Dubailand. These homes are at the forefront of an emerging destination for leisure, tourism, and entertainment. Located between Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Road and other major road networks, Q-Line connects you to the most amazing destinations within the emirate.

These contemporary apartments are available in studio, 1 and 2-bedroom units with magnificent interiors and surrounded by lush green landscapes. Each home in Q-line features large windows that allow an abundance of natural light. With spacious living rooms and superior finishes, one can enjoy a distinctive and satisfying lifestyle with Q-Line Apartments.
