Crystal Residence Apartments by Myra Properties

Crystal Residence is a residential project by Myra Properties located in the magnificent community of Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC). Each home is designed to embody comfort and style while being affordably priced for its location. Being nestled in the center of commercial and leisure facilities, Crystal Residence offers a real sense of community living. The homes are located near The Circle Mall which houses 200 shops, a supermarket, dining destinations, and a cinema.

With JVC now being considered as a new business district in Dubai, the residences are also best connected to commercial and trade, while promoting high quality living in the serene community. Crystal Residence provides a total of 25 residences ranging from studio, 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, and 3-bedroom apartments.
