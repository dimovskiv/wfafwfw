Marbella Villas by RAK Properties

Nestled in the stunning and magnificently serene islandic area of Hayat Island, Ras Al Khaimah. is quite obviously an urbane yet serenity and tranquility driven residential development comprising of 2BR, 3BR, 4BR & 5BR stunning villas and townhouses. Based in the breathtakingly splendid and mind-boggling community of Hayat Island Ras Al Khaimah, the development's infrastructure has been built and designed in an uber-contemporary yet comfort driven serene manner to be a fit with its surroundings. Everybody in the region is well-aware that Hayat Island is a multimillion-dollar residential as well as recreational development project located in the apex of one of the most commercially developed and popular areas of Emirate of Ras Al Khaimah i.e. Mina Al Arab. The development can be safely categorized as a mixed-use one as there are a number of kinds of properties it comprises of i.e. residential project, commercial development, retail and hospitality developments as well as some other off plan properties too. However, what it is most famous for is its provision of residential properties and it ever fails in standing true to its promise of delivering at par lifestyle to its occupants. The villas are all designed with immaculate intricacy and along the lines of a metropolitan yet islandic and secluded lifestyle approach that seconds the very idea of the community this residential developed is based in. RAK Properties is known to be a very trustable name in the business for years now and that is the very reason they have come up with another remarkable project in the shape of Marbella Villas serving as the pinnacle of comfortable lifestyle combined with unmatched finesse and intricacy in its overall design.

Marbella Villas Premium Amenities

Marbella Villas by RAK Properties, as the name of the development somewhat already implies, is a green development with big foliages and big green patches of lusciously built gardens surrounding all four sides of the homes within and one of the main attractions to this development is that the luxury houses are available at extremely affordable rates and easy payment plans. The villas and townhouses are all designed with utmost intricacy and along the lines of a metropolitan yet islandic and isolated lifestyle approach that seconds the very idea of the community this residential development is nestling in i.e. Hayat Island in Mina Al Arab Ras Al Khaimah, which is a very spacious and commodious development and has been specifically formed and designed to accommodate residential as well as globally renowned recreational ventures within it. The interiors of the villas and townhouses are master-planned to be spacious and designed in a way that makes them commodious for the residents to embellish according to their own tastes and preferences. Thus, it is certainly a major grab for families, couples, as well as group of people planning to live together alike since they can adorn, ornate and embellish their stunning houses at Marbella Villas Ras Al Khaimah as per their own convenience and do not have to make do with a pre-designed house.
