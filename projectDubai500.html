RP Heights Apartments by Ellington Properties

RP Heights by Ellington Properties is a 50-story apartment tower offering a luxurious lifestyle for people seeking upscale privileges. The premium apartments are located in Downtown Dubai which is the home of flagship stores of many leading brands. Residents can also easily access different cafe shops, dining restaurants, clubs, and other community places where anyone can spend quality time. The soaring tower of Ellington RP Heights has a unique and splendid architecture and design, embarked with an incredible view of North to South of Dubai Downtown.

Apart from watching the glorious panoramic landmarks of Dubai, any resident can take pride in living in such glamorous residences. The luxury residences are available in 1, 2 and 3-bedroom arrangements.
