District One Villas by District One

If you are looking for a beautiful and spacious residence for your growing family, look no more as District One Villas by District One will most definitely meet your expectations. Families that are looking for ample space can surely settle here as they are offering four (4) bedroom, five (5) bedroom, and six (6) bedroom villas with one-of-a-kind amenities in the city of Mohammed Bin Rashed (MBR), Dubai.

Redefine luxury with District One Villas as they have a selection of exquisite villas that will surely take your breath away. While the exteriors of the villa scream magnificent, the interiors, too, will leave you amazed as they boast of smart functions that make life much more convenient for both the adults and children.

Take note that the smallest space is sized at 6,144 square feet for four (4) bedroom villas. With that said, you can expect lots of space to move around within your home. With that said, you can unleash your creativity as you can have room to customize your space according to your taste — from deciding the purpose of a room to decorating it, you have the option to do anything you would like to do.

Lastly, you would like your home to be inviting — and by inviting, we mean aesthetically pleasing. As mentioned above, the exteriors of the villa is indeed magnificent. However, beauty does lie in the eye of the beholder; here, too, you can choose a villa that fits your idea of beauty perfectly.

Contemporary & Mediterranean

If you are looking into purchasing a villa that is minimalistic yet sleek and elegant, then you should opt with a Contemporary or a Mediterranean style villa. These two styles feature glass windows as well as sliding glass doors for a more nature-centered vibe. With natural light ruling over the home, your home will look wider create this sense of openness that will definitely keep you glued to your home for days.

Modern Arabic

Should you wish to live in a home that is more vibrant and cultural in nature, perhaps the Modern Arabic villas would be a better choice for you. These villas exude a strong sense of Arabian pride with its rich heritage and bold aesthetics and provide a sense of exclusivity and grandeur all throughout the place.

In addition to that, Modern Arabic homes are larger in size and should accommodate large families and guests that would need both privacy and comfort.

Premium Amenities

Some amenities that you can look forward to in the area are the following:

  • Fully-equipped gymnasium
  • Large swimming pool
  • Play area for children
  • State-of-the-art security system
  • Parking spaces in each villa
  • Wide balconies in each villa
  • 8-kilometer cycling and running track near the area
  • 600 acres of wide open space — perfect for hosting a get together;
  • 90-acre crystal lagoon

Prime Location

Once again, District One Villas can be found in Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum City. The residents of the area can easily access fantastic leisure and entertainment facilities including an IMAX Theatre, multiple sports areas, and of course, The Meydan Hotel — a hotel that is home to many recreational areas to keep everyone in the family occupied.
