Redwood Park Townhouses by Jumeirah Golf Estates

Redwood Park by Jumeirah Golf Estates developer is a selection of 75 premium townhouses at the Jumeirah Golf Estates development with spectacular views of the Fire golf course. The ready-to-move residences are nestled in lush green fairways and are available in 3 and 4-bedroom luxury townhouses. The residential project is designed with finishes inspired by Tuscan themes with superior quality materials.

Premium Amenities

Redwood Park Townhouses feature two and three-story residences, ensuring that layout puts bedrooms on the top floor for privacy. The homes feature roof decks which double as a private entertainment space in several homes, boasting of stunning golf course views. Materials like plaster, cast stone moldings, and burnt clay tiles are carefully chosen and used in Redwood Park Townhouses facade to keep the Tuscany ambience. The kitchens will be equipped with the finest European-inspired finishings like laminated, glossy lacquered or wood veneer finish combined with high-standard ironmongery. Residents of Redwood Park Townhouses can access premium amenities including a swimming pool and kids’ pool with golf course views, kids play areas, barbeque kiosk, and ample parking spaces.Â  Stroll around its paved walkways and lush fairways where you can admire natural sceneries and a fountain.

Convenient Location

Redwood Park Townhouses are located within the Jumeirah Golf Estates master development, a community rich in wildlife and is home about 400 types of birds. The coveted location features 15 magnificent lakes and guarantees a peaceful hill town living. Accessible within the area are 5 Andalusian heritage sites within the area that allows you to get connected with history and culture. The neighborhood of Redwood Park Townhouses is regarded as one of the greenest and most sought after residential locations in the UAE.
