Mira Oasis II by Emaar Properties

A part of the mesmerizing residential development of Reem by Emaar Properties, Mira Oasis II is a blueprint for the urban family community, comprising beautifully designed cluster townhouses and terrace houses. Contemporary 21st century living at its best, Reem breaks new ground with a host of avant-garde retail, recreation, and leisure offerings on your doorstep. The townhouses and terrace houses open into vast spreading foliages engulfing the houses on all sides providing the residents with ultimate proximity to nature. Taking the quintessential western and urban approach to the lifestyle, all the houses possess their own outdoor courtyards perfect to make them feel at home and breathe freely. Essentially, over and beyond everything, Mira Oasis II is a secured and gated community providing protection to the residents not only physically but also giving them the peace of mind.

Premium Amenities

Due to its central location in Reem Community, Mira Oasis II has easy access to a number of different amenities. At its nucleus, Reem’s shopping avenue and entertainment zones are the pulse of this satellite community. A radial network of tree-lined boulevards with dedicated pedestrian and cyclist paths provide residents seamless access to a plethora of world-class amenities. Driven by a family-centric mantra, Reem offers six million square feet of the city’s finest leisure, entertainment, retail and social facilities for a spectrum of ages and choices. Apart from the community hub, residential pockets also enjoy neighbourhood swimming pools, sports courts, retail centres, and pocket parks as well as mosques and schools
