Symphony by Gemini Property Developers

Symphony by Gemini Property Developers is a premium residential tower located in the heart of BusinessÂ  Bay. The 29-storey building (G+29) offers a selection of studio, 1, 2 and 3-bedroom apartments with astounding views of Burj Khalifa, the Marasi Promenade and Downtown Dubai, which illuminate magnificence, especially at night.

Premium Amenities

The residences of Symphony are designed with efficient layouts that will surely allow you to enjoy the comfort of home. Symphony offers the finest amenities to complete your dream living experience. Be welcomed by the efficient concierge at the lobby for any assistance needed. The living spaces are equipped with a Smart Home system, advanced technology that provides absolute convenience.

If you are the type who aims to keep your active and healthy lifestyle, maintain your fitness routine within its state of the art gym and yoga lawn. You could also take a run in jogging track within the neighborhood. If you wish to have leisure time after a long day at work, you can take a swim in its nice swimming pool. Kids can also have their time to unwind at the play area, while you can accommodate your visitors for a good time over food by the barbecue area.

Convenient Location

Located in one of the main business districts in the Emirate, Symphony by Gemini Property Developers is set at the forefront of international and local businesses, financial institutions, retail and hospitality options. The residences can be easily accessed through the Sheikh Zayed and Al Khail Road. The homes can be also reached with a 10-minute drive from the Dubai International Airport.
