Zabeel Saray Royal Residences by Meraas Holding

Zabeel Saray Royal Residences is a premier residential project located in the crescent of the stunning Palm Jumeirah development. The royal residences are clustered around a stunning lagoon pool and set amid lush tropical gardens, all inspired by the Turkish Ottoman style lush tropical gardens. Zabeel Saray Royal Residences are composed 38 residential villas with direct beach access and amazing views of the Arabian Gulf from the balcony.

Premium Amenities

The luxury villas of Zabeel Saray Royal Residences are designed with the finest materials including Turkish marble, rich dark wood and exterior ceramics from the ancient town of Iznik in Western Anatolia. The upscale homes are designed with the highest specifications and intricate details, complementing its beautiful beachfront and the wealth of 5-star facilities. There are three types of villas featured by the project: Beach Royal Residences, Seafront Royal Residences, and Lagoon Royal Residences.

Beachfront Royal Residences feature luxury style homes with stunning views of the Gulf, spacious living areas, private pool, fully-equipped kitchens, maid’s quarters, private parking, private entrance, and a private elevator. Its nice garden is a great venue for romantic dinners of family bondings over food. The homes showcase dark wood furnishings with Turkish motifs in ivory and gold hues. Beachfront Royal Residences are available in 5-bedroom villas.

Seafront Royal Residences overlook landscaped gardens and views of the Gulf. The homes offer the same features as the Beachfront Royal Residences with Ottoman-inspired furnishings and Turkish-style furniture. Seafront Royal Residences are available in 5-bedroom villas.

Convenient Location

The strategic location of Zabeel Saray Royal Residences is just 15 minutes away from the Central Business District and 25 minutes away from the Dubai Airport. Access a wide range of amenities within the community, in addition to hotel services provided in every home. The premier tourist destination of Palm Jumeirah is considered as one the finest locations to live in Dubai, a beach community with easy access to major road networks in the emirate.
