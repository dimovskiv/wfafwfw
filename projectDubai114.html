Marsa Al Arab Villas by Dubai Holding

Marsa Al Arab Villas is a set of two gated and secured island in the beautiful and vibrant community of  Jumeirah next to Burj Al Arab. This immaculate development is also a vibrant and upbeat city in all its grandeur featuring top amenities and facilities. It is part of an exclusive collection of 9 mansions villas comprise of 6 Bedrooms villa with a stunning 360-degree view of the sea and direct access to the beach. Located on the coastline directly alongside Jumeirah Beach Hotel, the mansions are spread over 15,000 sq. ft. plot sizes and across three floors that includes a semi-basement and floor-to-ceiling windows, which allows residents to take in the full view of the Arabian Sea. Marsa Al Arab by Dubai Holding is being built on a man-made island extending out from the coast, giving it an ideal waterfront setting. The whole community and city of Marsa Al Arab comprise of various amenities and hide-outs in the vicinity of this vibrant neighborhood. It comprises of an all-encompassing community center, play areas, parks and luscious open areas, fitness centers and swimming pools, as well as ample space for basement parking for residents, also features designated parking spaces for guests.
