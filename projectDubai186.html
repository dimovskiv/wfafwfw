La Cote Residences by Meraas Holding

La Cote at Port De La Mer is Meraa’s most recent addition to its existing chain of the most astounding & revolutionary residential projects. Amalgamating luxurious living with a comfortable lifestyle bearing access to all the defining necessities of life with 1BR, 2BR, 3BR & 4BR apartments and a dedicated space for townhouses, La Cote at Port De La Mer is surely one of the most anticipated ventures in the region. This immaculate development comprises of 5 residential buildings with a total of 408 intricately designed and adorned low rise apartments serving as the canvass of some of the state-best interior designers. All the apartments own their separate out-looking balconies providing a beautiful view of the stunning Port De La Mer in the surroundings.

La Cote at Port De La Mer – Balcony with Amazing Views

Premium Amenities

The residential community of La Cote at Port De La Mer possesses a plethora of some of the finest amenities. Premium facilities like large swimming pools, a dedicated gym, a spacious beach and marina access as well as proximity to the best set of hotels, retail outlets, diners, and restaurants are some of the key location pluses that residents can enjoy at Port De La Mer. In addition to all these facilities, the residential development entails bustling Piazzas and conveniently located bridges to help residents travel easily within the community.

La Cote at Port De La Mer – Swimming Pool

Convenient Location

Port De La Mer, being a port is accessible both through the road and sea. Enjoying a very accessible and central location, La Cote at Port De La Mer lies in close proximity to Meraas at a mere 5 minute distance, at just a 10 minute distance from the famous City Walk by Meraas, a 15 minute drive from Dubai International Airport, and a 45 minute distance from Al Maktoum International Airport. This means that residents can enjoy feasible access to the main attractions of the city as well as enjoy a breathtaking view of the coastline.

Features

Inspired by the allure of the Mediterranean, Port de La Mer offers panoramic views of the open sea and Dubai skyline, it is truly a one-of-a-kind island retreat within the city. The residential community comprises of spectacular facilities and amenities, located at the tip of the north peninsula at La Mer.

  • Seaside promenades overlooking Pearl Jumeira
  • +190 berth marina and yacht club
  • Beach access
  • Dining, shopping, and leisure experiences
  • Hotels and resorts in the vicinity
  • Outdoor park and play area
  • Landscaped terraces
  • Swimming pools
