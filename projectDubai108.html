Liwan Phase I (Plot) by Dubai Holding

Liwan Phase 1 is part of Dubailand properties and is surrounded by numerous developments. Its prime location makes it a hotspot for a number of developments. Situated at the crossroads of Mohammed Bin Zayed Road and Al Ain Road, Liwan Phase 1 is interconnected to a number of sites which makes it accessible in all areas. The open spaces in Liwan Phase 1 can accommodate various activities and offer an active lifestyle with a relaxed setting.

Created as a mixed-use community, Liwan Phase 1 is built primarily for residential, recreational, business and retail-related components. Liwan Phase 1 is located with interesting topography allowing for elevated sites and undulating open spaces. The master plan provides two main activity nodes with mixed-use elements. Liwan Phase 1 is spread over a site area of nearly 14 million square feet with over 15% open area and includes a community of 100 residential complexes, 6 business towers, and entertainment.
