Mediterranean Tower- Canal Residence West by Dubai Sports City

Canal Residence West – Mediterranean Tower by Dubai Sports City (DSC) developer is an 11-storey residential building counted as one of the five district towers located in the Dubai Sports City development. The Mediterranean Tower is a ready-to-move tower which was completed in 2013 (other ready towers are European and Venetian towers). Only the Canal Residence West – Arabian Tower and its Spanish Tower are still undergoing construction. The Canal Residence West – Mediterranean Tower features 12 floors with studio, 1, 2 and 3 bedroom apartments. These magnificent living spaces will be showcasing stunning views of the golf course, canal, and Sports Village. These residences are among the most sought-after homes in Sports City with high capital and rental values.

Premium Amenities

The modern building of Canal Residence West – Mediterranean Tower features classic architectural finishes. The residences are complete with high ceilings and wide living spaces, equipped with an open plan kitchen, fitted cabinets, attached bathrooms, and a wide balcony. Canal Residence West – Mediterranean Tower is equipped with state-of-the-art amenities such as a high-end gym, swimming pool, and parking spaces. The tower is secured with 24-hour security system.

Convenient Location

Canal Residence West – Mediterranean Tower is located in the Dubai Sports City development. The global sporting venue is also a beautiful residential community consist of mid-rise apartment buildings, villas and high-end sports facilities including an international golf course and a cricket stadium. Accessible within the area are international schools, world-class medical facilities, hotels, leisure destinations, and even lifestyle country clubs. The location of Canal Residence West – Mediterranean Tower will be home to a sports-themed retail mall with a leasing capacity of approximately 1.2 million square feet, where a wide selection of shops, markets, retail plazas, and boutiques can be seen.
