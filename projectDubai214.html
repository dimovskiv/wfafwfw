K1 Residence by Time Properties

K1 Residence is a seven-storey residential project located in Dubai Residential Complex by featuring studio and one-bedroom apartments. The building is structured with a basement floor, ground floor, 6 floors and a terrace on the 7th floor. The first floor of the K1 Residence has 22 studio units and 4 one-bedroom units, while the second floor to the sixth floor has 18 studio units and 6 one-bedroom units each floor. The residences are nestled on wide green areas, ensuring the right nature elements combine with city living. The K1 Residence is expected to be handover on the 4th quarter of 2019. This project is exclusively sold by 5 Star Real Estate.

K1 Residence – Night View

The luxury apartments would be a good property investment, being the only project with a very affordable price range within Dubailand. This new concept of private high-end residences in a town is seen to a project with an incredible growth potential.

Premium Amenities

K1 Residence is equipped with the finest amenities that will definitely make you want to enjoy living. Access the finest amenities which includes a fitness center, a separate gym for men and women, perfect for maintaining an active lifestyle. These gyms will be located on the topmost floor with separate male and female restrooms. You can also take a dip in its lovely swimming pool for your personal leisure time or bonding with the family. The residences also have ample parking spaces with the ground floor accommodating 34 vehicles and the podium floor spacious enough to have room for 44 vehicles.

Convenient Location

K1 Residence is located in the Dubai Residential Complex, regarded as one of the new emerging locations in Dubai. The area has direct access to the Al Ain Road and the Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Road, and just 13 minutes away from Emirates Road allowing easy access to various parts of Dubai. Easily travel to Dubai Mall in just 15 minutes, 10 minutes to Dubai Internet City and Motor City and just 20 minutes to Dubai International Airport. The Dubai Residential Complex is also close to public transportation stops being just 20 minutes away from the Rashidiya Metro Station.
