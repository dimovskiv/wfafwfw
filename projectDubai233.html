The Residences JLT by Signature Developers

The Residences JLT is a grand residential project by Signature Developers located in the Jumeirah Lakes Towers development. The homes are available in three different collections namely The Monogram Collection, The Autograph Collection, and The Signature Collection all residing in the same tower of the Taj Hotel, which occupies 18 storeys of the building.

The Monogram Collection is located between the floors 20th to 30th of the building, available in 53 two and three-bedroom apartments. The residences feature views of the JLT, Emirates Hills, Dubai Marina, and Downtown Dubai.

The Autograph Collection is located in the 31st to 36th floors and the 38th, 40th and 42nd floors of the building, featuring 21 three, four and five bedroom residences.

The Signature Collection is located on levels 37 to 42 of the building, with 3 full floor penthouses on levels 37, 39 and 41 and signature 5-bedroom apartments on levels 38, 40 and 42. The residences offer amazing views of the Emirates Hills neighborhood and the Dubai Marina.

Premium Amenities

The Residences JLT feature unique designs for each collection, all ensuring elegance and sophistication.

The Monogram Collection features contemporary living spaces with ambient light, marble floors and avant-garde technology best for relaxation. The unique homes offer a cavernous ensuite master bedroom, spacious ensuite bedrooms with smooth interiors and floor-to-ceiling windows. The homes have a 3-meter ceiling height while its flooring is designed with marble finishes. The Monogram Collection of The Residences JLT also feature open balconies, terraces and floating glass pods.

The Autograph Collection features luxury living spaces inspired with cosmopolitan flair and accents. he homes feature patterned marble floors, expansive floor-to-ceiling windows, a ceiling height of up to 4 meters, with a separate family lounge. The minimalist designs by The Autography Collection in The Residences JLT are seen in its soft, ambient light and subtle textures.

The Signature Collection features clean-lined residences with an astounding view of the city with contemporary-themed interiors and detailed fittings. The homes are designed with marble floors with up to 3.6 meters of ceiling heights. The Signature Collection from The Residences JLT features three-side open balconies and terraces with large living and dining areas ideal for entertainment.

All three collections feature stylish kitchens equipped with premium appliances. The bathrooms are designed with elegant bathtubs and integrated vanity mirrors.

Easily access a wide range of amenities within the luxury building including an elegant lobby, a fully-equipped fitness center with steam and sauna facilities, a multipurpose room that is ideal for intimate gatherings and parties, a yoga room, swimming pool and a treatment room for a relaxing massage after a long day.

Convenient Location

The Residences JLT is situated in the sought-after community of Jumeirah Lakes Towers. The area is designed with nature-inspired landscapes, man-made lakes, and waterways. Residents can easily access retail outlets, dining areas and several leisure facilities in a vibrant community. The location is also seen as an emerging business area with various office buildings within the vicinity. The neighborhood of Jumeirah Lakes Towers is easily accessible through the Jumeirah Lakes Towers Metro Station.
