Marina Arcade by Mada’in Properties

Marina Arcade by Mada’in Properties is a stunning residential tower in Dubai Marina with amazing views of the Palm Jumeirah, Dubai Media City, Jumeirah Beach, and the Emirates Golf Course. The ready a-to-move in residences are available in 1, 2, 3 and 4 bedroom apartments, as well as a selection signature apartments and duplex apartments. The premier homes are designed by multi-award winning Italian interior designer Matteo Nunziati.

Premium Amenities

Marina Arcade apartments are designed with the ambience of relaxation and well-being. Each home is designed with large and open interiors that give the best views of the community. The features a wealth of exciting amenities. Marina Arcade is crowned with top-tier, 24-hour concierge and valet services that guarantee utmost convenience. Keep you physique in shape with easy access to health and fitness centers within the tower, all equipped with the latest gym equipment. In fact, Marina Arcade has a 6,000 square feet fitness center, which is the largest in the Marina. The homes are designed with exclusive elevators which leads all the way to a majestic rooftop garden. Enjoy a relaxing time on a private jacuzzi or in its swimming pools. You can also step outside the home and head down the track for a good morning jog in its dedicated running track.

Convenient Location

The iconic architectural gem of Marina Arcade is located in the astonishing Dubai Marina community. The vibrant waterfront neighborhood does not only exude beauty in its surroundings but also provides the finest restaurants and leisure amenities within its vicinity. Aside from that, Marina Arcade is also in close proximity to main road networks and even public transportation links which makes travelling to other parts of Dubai such ease. The stunning beaches are just a few steps from home, where nature and modern living blends perfectly. Play a game of golf just across the road or enjoy its al fresco bistros and vibrant late night spots.
