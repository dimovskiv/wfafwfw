I Love Florence Tower by Dar Al Arkan

I Love Florence Tower Apartments by Dar-Al-Arkan is a range of vibrant apartments in Business Bay Dubai, with interior design by famous fashion czar Roberto Cavalli. The residences reflect the unique and artful creations of the Cavalli brand which contributes to the holistic architectural beauty of the homes. The 34-storey of the I Love Florence Tower features one, two, three and four-bedroom apartments.

Premium Amenities

The residences of I Love Florence Tower boasts of stunning views of Dubai Water Canal or the Burj Khalifa as well as panoramic vistas of the city from your balcony.  Once you step inside of its premises, you will be welcomed by a sophisticated lobby with flowing and elegant designs. Every living space is inspired with Italian finishes that showcase both artistic brilliance and comfortable privacy. The residences have a private elevator that opens into your living room that showcases views of the Dubai Canal. The bedrooms exude a warm ambience that allows your mind to relax. The bathrooms are decorated with impeccable designs making it the perfect space to get rejuvenated.  Cook the finest dishes in its state-of-the-art kitchen that are both spacious and stylish, combining unique and urban accents.

Access an expansive swimming pool at the podium level and stroll along its Florence-inspired city gardens and water fountains. With a fully-equipped gym, one can maintain a healthy and fit lifestyle with the help of the best fitness trainers. I Love Florence Tower also provides 50 grand parking spaces.

Stunning Location

I Love Florence Tower is located at the heart of Business Bay, Dubai. The modern metropolis keeps you connected with a wealth of the destinations within the city. Walk along its azure waterways and the Dubai Water Canal. The finest entertainment, fine dining and leisure destinations just a few blocks away from home. Also surrounding the area by a line of luxury hotels including JW Marriott Marquiz, The Westin Dubai, St. Regis Dubai and W Dubai. I Love Florence Tower is just a few minutes away from Dubai Mall, City Walk, Burj Khalifa and the Meydan Racecourse.
