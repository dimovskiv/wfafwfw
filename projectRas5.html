Al Mahra Resort by Fam Holding is an elegant hotel project located in the Al Marjan Island . The premier hospitality tower features studio, 1, 2 and 3 bedroom hotel units nestled on the stunning beach of the coral-shaped island in Ras Al Khaimah.

Al Mahra Resort Premium Amenities

The Al Mahra Resort provides the finest amenities that all guarantee a luxury living experience. Walk in to its stylish lobby designed with contemporary finishes, which welcomes you to its VIP check-in areas.
