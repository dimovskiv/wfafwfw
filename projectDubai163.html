Belgravia Square by Ellington Properties

Belgravia Square by Ellington Properties is a residential tower located at the heart of Jumeirah Village Circle. A subtle grey Oakwood building standing tall in the flourishing neighborhood of Jumeirah Village Circle, Belgravia Square stands donning two L shaped blocks facing each other. The development offers Studio, 1BR and 2BR apartments with dedicated parking spaces for tenants. All the units within the tower are built in congruent color themes and patterns that are surely soothing to live in. This freehold development has been designed by some of the best designers and engineers in the business and Ellington Properties has again not compromised on its standards when it comes to combining elegance, class and value for money. The grey facade of the building provides the residents with an ultimate sense of serenity and a truly acoustic experience. All the primary interior appliances i.e. kitchen deck, sinks, faucets etc are of European high brands. All the residences are pre-fitted with high-end wood cabinets and walk-in closets and also securely pre-wired and covered to prevent exposure to kids. The residential development is surrounded by a larger than life beautifully landscaped garden providing a pretty view and access to those post-work me-time evening strolls.

Belgravia Square in JVC – Bedroom

PREMIUM AMENITIES

Belgravia Square is a spacious development designed to capacitate all the superior amenities like a gigantic swimming pool, a properly guarded and covered kids’ play area and imaginative spaces designed to take children into a wondrous little world of creative imagination, a dedicated meditative outdoor yoga area for adults and a sports and fitness centre fitted with world-class high-tech equipments within it. The residential development also features a chic residential lounge within the facility where residents can sit, relax and lounge as well. Apart from all these amazing facilities, the overall view and vibe of the tower itself is fairly tranquilizing with some of the best contemporary art pieces hanging down the walls in the hallways and lobby.

Belgravia Square in JVC – Pool Area

CONVENIENT LOCATION

Belgravia Square is situated at an ideal location in Jumeirah Village Circle in terms of its proximity to some of the landmark locations of the Emirate. The place has close access to all the touch points that contribute to making a decision in terms of living i.e. retail outlets, healthcare options, schools and offices, transportation links etc. The development project is notably close to Burj Khalifa, Downtown Dubai and Mall of the Emirates that make it a central location.
