Hameni Homes by Zaya Property Developers

Built in the popular neighborhood of Jumeirah Village Circle, Hameni Homes by Zaya Property Developers is a luxurious residential development that offers deluxe penthouses with ornate interior and lifestyle facilities to residents. It provides the residents with a plethora of options to chose from i.e. 2 Bed Duplex, 3 Bed Simplex, 3 Bed Duplex, 4 Bed and 4 Bedroom Penthouse Apartments are the wide housing options one can select from. All the apartments are built along a uniform color pattern of grey and beige both signifying tranquility and peace. Hameni Homes- a 31 storey residential tower stretching out to Dubai’s much-famed skyline is a perfect epitome of urban-lifestyle. The exquisite penthouses have access to beautiful day-light and natural air through the intricately built floor to ceiling glass windows that lead out to private balconies for each apartment. Exclusive to the residents, a podium terrace serves as an open-air deck too spacious to incorporate planting as well as seating space whichever way the residents desire to decorate it. Thus, it would be safe to say that Hameni Homes are built in perfect balance in terms of exposure to a suburban as well as a naturally sustainable way of life. The internal water-management and sound-maintenance systems are also meticulously engineered within the apartments to avoid noise pollution and provide clean drinking water to the tenants. In addition to this, the prevention of the passage of toxic air is made sure through the usage of low VOC paints used within the houses. Internally available healthy eating options is one unique feature offered to people opting to live within the immaculately built Hameni Homes.

Hameni Homes by Zaya – Panoramic Views

PREMIUM AMENITIES

Hameni Homes by Zaya have their own beautiful relaxing SPA that provides rejuvenation to the residents. A child’s wellbeing is a parents’ utmost worry and Hameni has that covered for them in the form of ‘Petit-Z Club’- a childcare center and nursery that doesn’t only take care of the kids but also build and groom their personalities creatively and morally so that they grow up into responsible and good-natured individuals. The luscious pool within Hameni Homes is its biggest attraction to the residents. It is a 60-foot infinity lap pool shadowed with private cabanas and stylish sun-loungers. In addition to these, a pool bar, a healthy-eating restaurant, and a properly facilitated gymnasium are also some of the supremely built amenities provided to residents.

Hameni Homes by Zaya – Open Space

CONVENIENT LOCATION

Jumeirah Village Circle has already established itself as one of the mainstream and prosperous areas in the vicinity and is at a central location. Hameni Homes is located between Al Khail and Sheikh Zayed Road on Hessa Street which puts it in close proximity to hotels, schools, malls as well as metro links and bus stops. Mall of The Emirates, Downtown, Sheikh Zayed Road, Marina and Dubai International Airport are the best locations located close to Hameni Homes by Zaya in JVC. In terms of schools, some of the best schools in the region like Dubai British School, Dubai International Academy, American School of Dubai etc are all located at a 15-minute distance to the residential towers.
