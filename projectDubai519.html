Dubai Wharf

Dubai Wharf is one of the best contemporary luxury apartments by leading developer Dubai Properties. The project is a mix-used development set within Dubai Creek that features high-quality luxury apartments. Dubai Wharf has 594 residential units and about 150 retail, dining and entertainment units on the ground floor and first floor. The residences also feature ample car parking spaces at the basement level.
