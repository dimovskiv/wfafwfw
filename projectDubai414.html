Hayat Boulevard by NSHAMA

Hayat Boulevard is a stunning mid-rise apartment tower located in the world-class city of Nshama Town Square. The apartments offer a variety of sizes and arrangements that include 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, and 3-bedroom units. Each unit boasts a magnificent view of lush green surroundings and healthy ambience, as the apartment structure was built using eco-friendly architecture. Hayat Boulevard is also equipped with high-end recreational facilities at the podium level. The apartment features a well-organized commercial space, and key amenities like a health clinic, fine-dining restaurant, nursery, and school, state-of-the-art gym and a variety of shopping areas around the vicinity. Hayat Boulevard offers round-the-clock facilities including security, electricity, and recreational activities along with unconditional amusing facilities.
