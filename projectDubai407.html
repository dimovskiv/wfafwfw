Mont Rose by Deyaar Properties

Mont Rose is a three-tower apartment complex located the beautiful community of Barsha South. Each home exudes sheer elegance and functionality, being equipped with modern smart home technology. The exquisite residences by Dubai-based developer Deyaar are nestled in one of the greenest communities in the emirate.

Mont Rose offers a total of 286 units available in studio, 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, 3-bedroom, and 4-bedroom arrangements. Each of the three towers has 19 storeys and has three basement levels. Two of its residential towers are composed of 68 one-bedroom, 68 two-bedroom, 5 three-bedroom, and 2 four bedroom units. Its 3rd tower, meanwhile, has 88 studios, 68 one bedroom, and 24-two bedroom units.
