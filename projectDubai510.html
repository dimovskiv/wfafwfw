The Opus Residence by Omniyat

The Opus Residence is a luxury residential tower located in the modern community of Business Bay, one of UAE’s central business districts. The towers, both The Opus residential and commercial, are designed by renowned world-class architect Zaha Hadid. Aside from its iconic glowing cube at the center of the towers, The Opus Residence features the best amenities and astonishing waterfront views, as well as vistas of the Burj Dubai area, the Dancing Tower and the Dubai Financial Market. The Opus Residence offers apartments (and a selection of hotel units) operated by the leading hotelier group ME by Melia Hotels International. Every unit is designed with the finest interiors and choice materials that only promote the ultimate luxury experience.

The Opus Residence

These residences are available in 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, 2-bedroom duplex, and 3-bedroom triplex offer a 2-years post-handover payment plan.

Premium Amenities

The Opus Residence will give every resident everything they need from ME Hotel’s first-class services, which delivers a la carte / pre-selected services that all cater to your personal needs. From its carefully crafted landscaped designs that complement the tower itself, to its welcoming entrance and lobby, you will surely feel at home in its calm and unobtrusive ambience. Each home is designed with extraordinary amenities including a 24/7 security service, valet parking, and concierge. Residents and guests of The Opus Residence will also have access to Michelin star restaurants, lounges, and cafes that occupies about 250,000 square feet with entertainment options. Other upscale facilities within the tower include temperature-controlled swimming pools, state-of-the-art gymnasium, world-class spa and business center facilities.

The Opus Residence – Swimming Pool

Convenient Location

The Opus Residence is located in strategically in the vibrant and modern commercial hub of Business Bay. The area is renowned as an ideal location for regional and international headquarters of large companies in UAE, being situated off the major thoroughfare Sheikh Zayed Road (between interchanges 1 and 2). Yet aside from its city vibe, the area will be incorporating natural elements such as landscaped gardens and water features.
