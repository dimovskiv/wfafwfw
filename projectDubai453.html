Bay’s Edge Luxury Hotel Apartments by Damac Properties

DAMAC Maison Bay’s Edge is a mixed-use luxury hotel apartment tower that combines serviced living and premium hotels. Located in Business Bay, which is considered as the ‘platinum square kilometre’ of Dubai, the development represents the epitome of quality living and the ultimate lucrative investment opportunity. The beautiful tower is set near in the iconic Burj District of Dubai and within a short distance to some of the world’s most amazing attractions, including the Burj Khalifa, dancing fountains, Dubai Mall and an entire neighbourhood of stunning avenues.

Your suite at DAMAC Maison Bay’s Edge brings together the luxuries of a world-class hotel with the homely comforts of family living. The furnished properties are complete with state-of-art amenities and all the services you would expect from a luxury hotel stay. You can see attention-to-detail in every aspect of this luxury icon; be it the architectural finesse, thoughtful space allocation or the endless luxurious amenities.
