Jumeirah Living Marina Gate by Select Group

Jumeirah Living Marina Gate by Select Group is a freehold development located in Marina Gate which serves as the entrance of Dubai Marina. The residences present in the community all embody perfect luxury, as all boast of magnificent views of the palm-lined promenade.

Boasting of the ultimate Marina lifestyle, these homes provide the best quality residences and outstanding amenities to the convenience of its residents. Jumeirah Living Marina Gate are available in studio. 1, 2, 3 and 4-bedroom arrangements with impeccable finishes and comfortable living spaces at par to Marina living standards. Each villa is also furnished with A-grade kitchen appliances.

With valet parking services and a 24-hour concierge service, resents of Jumeirah Living Marina Gate are sure to enjoy their everyday living experience.
