Marasi Business Bay Water Homes by Dubai Properties

Marasi Business Bay is the first range of luxury water homes in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) located in the heart of trade and development in Dubai. The project is designed to be a future major landmark in the emirate.

These elegant residences by Dubai Properties are located in the longest promenade in the UAE, which spans 12 kilometers. Marasi Business Bay Water Homes are divided into three main areas: The Yacht Club, The Park, and The Pier, designed to provide entertainment and leisure experiences.

Offering 2, 3 and 4-bedroom residences, the luxurious homes are perfectly set within an array of floating gourmet restaurants, high-end leisure facilities, shopping outlets and 700 huge yacht berths over stunning marinas lined with gorgeous palm trees.

Features and Amenities

  • Private Marina Berth
  • Close To Shopping Malls
  • Private Balcony Pools
  • Beach Side Waterfront
  • Easy Accessibility to Restaurants Hotels, Stores, etc.
