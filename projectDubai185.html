Azizi Fawad Residence by Azizi Developments

Situated in the heart of Dubai Healthcare City, Azizi Fawad Residence is the ultimate residential and retail development. A project of the renowned Azizi Developers, Azizi Fawad Residence yet again lives up to the very standard the development entity has succeeded in creating over the years. Comprising of an immaculately crafted studio, 1BR, and 2BR apartments, Azizi Fawad Residence is the perfect epitome of warmth amalgamated with comfortable living environment. All the apartments within the residential block are well ornate and embellished. Adorned with cutting-edge technological facilities and donning a classic touch simultaneously, the apartments all contain floor to ceiling thick glass reflective windows and beautiful glass doors to them. The kitchens are already fully equipped and well-planned in terms of managing space and storage resorts. All the apartments within Azizi Fawad Residence feature warm color tones to provide the houses with a hint of serenity and tranquility after a long tiring day at work.

Fawad Azizi – Spectacular Views of the CityScape

Premium Amenities

Azizi Fawad Residence features some state-of-the-art amenities for the residents coming from all different walks of life. Due to the residential development’s close proximity to Dubai Healthcare City, residents can avail a number of different recreational and leisure facilities at large. However, despite that, the development features a number of exciting amenities within the living space too i.e. clean and huge swimming pools, healthcare centers, kids play areas, fitness centers and gym etc. The vicinity also comprises of a beautiful town garden for residents to enjoy evening walks in. Coming to retail opportunities, the vicinity engulfs a number of different options in the retail arena too.

Fawad Azizi – Living Room

Convenient Location

Azizi Fawad Residence by Azizi Developers is situated in the heart of Dubai Healthcare City which is accessible through two major roads in the city i.e. Al Khail Road and Oud Metha Road. This close connection makes the residential development a hot spot in terms of various retail, leisure, dining and recreation opportunities it has access to. The residential block lies in close proximity to various commotion touch points too i.e. cab stands, bus stops, metro stations etc.
