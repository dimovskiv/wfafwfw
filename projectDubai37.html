Switzerland Island Villas by The Heart of Europe

An epitome of grandeur and splendor, Switzerland Island is a beach-facing villa developed by The Heart of Europe at the prestigious address of The World Islands. Offering an array of 2 bedroom chalet, 3 bedroom chalet, 3 suite beachfront villa, 3 bedroom cliff chalet, 4 bedroom cliff penthouse, 5 suite beachfront villa and 5 bedroom beachfront villa with pristine views of the waterfront. Switzerland Island is an embodiment of luxury, it is located near the beach and world-class dining outlets, offering unique amenities and facilities. Residents will surely be marveled with the opulent design and affluent lifestyle. Having a breathtaking view of the beach, the connection with nature is strong in such a mountainous country, whether winter or summer, and the fresh air and spectacular scenery are cornerstones of Swiss living.

Premium Amenities

All villas in Switzerland Island boast of views of the sea and upper floor suites with their own private rooftop plunge pools. Among its wide range of amenities is a dedicated marina, European style cafes and beach restaurants, spa and wellness center, luxury boutiques as well as interactive clubs for children of varying ages. Also access beautiful courtyards, swimming pools, landscaped gardens, white beach, a club for kids, watersports, diving and snorkeling facilities.

Switzerland Island is a sophisticated distillation of the country’s thirst for nature and extraordinary experiences. With 100+ units, it’s the perfect destination to slow down, relax and explore the best outdoor living can offer.

An ideal location

Switzerland Island is situated in the magnificent island development of The World Islands, a collection of islands intended to imitate the shape of the countries of the world. The premier hotel suites are strategically tucked away from the bustling noise of the city, making it a convenient place of escape for relaxation and leisure. Residents and tourists alike can access a wealth of premium amenities within the island neighborhood at your convenience. The World Islands has become renowned and tagged among the most popular island destinations in the world.
