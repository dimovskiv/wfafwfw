CÔTE D’AZUR Hotel by The Heart of Europe

CÔTE D’AZUR Hotel by The Heart of Europe is one of the most vibrant luxury hospitality project located in the magnificent The World Islands development. The residences are almost synonymous with LA Côte d’Azur which will also feature impressive architecture and design of Southern Europe. Own a luxury hotel unit and get guaranteed 100% return of investment (ROI) for its project up to 10 years.

Premium Amenities

CÔTE D’AZUR Hotel is composed of 839 rooms, suites, and deluxe apartments with balconies designed with Mediterranean finishes. With its high-quality materials and state-of-the-art technology, anyone who will stay in CÔTE D’AZUR Hotel will be embraced and surrounded by utmost convenience. The premium location has a wide range of shopping areas, restaurants, and Mediterranean-style beach cafes will offer every resident the finest services they could ever imagine. Also access beautiful courtyards, swimming pools, landscaped gardens, white beach, a club for kids, watersports, diving and snorkeling facilities.

Stunning Location

CÔTE D’AZUR Hotel is situated in the magnificent island development of The World Islands, a collection of islands intended to imitate the shape of the countries of the world. The premier hotel suites are strategically tucked away from the bustling noise of the city, making it a convenient place of escape for relaxation and leisure. Residents and tourists alike can access a wealth of premium amenities within the island neighborhood at your convenience. The World Islands has become one of the most popular and coveted island destinations internationally.
