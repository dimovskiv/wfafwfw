Act One | Act Two Tower II by Emaar Properties

Act One Act Two Tower II by Emaar Properties are exceptional residential towers situated in the urban master development of Downtown Dubai. These premium residences showcase stunning views of the Burj Khalifa and the magnificent cityscape. The residential project ‘s design is inspired by the distinct features of the dancing Dubai Fountain. Act One Act Two Tower II apartments are available in 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, and 3-bedroom units with contemporary interiors that perfectly combines comfort and luxury.

This opulent residential project is now offering 3 YEARS POST HANDOVER PAYMENT PLAN, making the residents purchase the property through a flexible payment schedule with easy installments.

Premium Amenities

Enjoy a one-of-a-kind high-rise living experience as the buildings of Act One Act Two Tower II act as a gateway for Burj Khalifa, with their podium connection acting as a frame. The residences feature a wealth of premium amenities within its premises. Keep your body in shape with its fully-equipped gymnasium and health club. If you’re looking for an exciting food adventure, choose from a wide selection of high-end restaurants and coffee shops in the high-end vicinity.

Stunning Location

Act One Act Two Tower II is strategically located in the heart of Downtown Dubai which is in close proximity to major road networks including Sheikh Zayed Road and Al Khail Road. The homes are also just a few minutes away from the Business Bay area, making sure that residents which is one the best central business hubs in the emirate.
