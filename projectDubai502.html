Hartland Greens Apartments by Sobha Group

Hartland Greens Apartments by Sobha Group offers a range of luxury apartments catering to both individuals and families looking for ideal homes in Dubai. Each apartment offers wide living spaces that are sure to give residents comfort and peace of mind. Hartland Greens Apartments, located in the Sobha Hartland development in Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum City, is an eight-story tower with garden apartments reflecting classic modernity.

The residences are available in sizes ranging from studio, 3 bedroom single-story apartments, as well as 2 and 3 bedroom duplexes on the upper floors. Residents are sure to experience unrivaled waterfront city-side living in the mixed-use development, as well as views of the Dubai Water Canal. The location of Hartland Greens Apartments also allows easy access to the city’s main attractions, restaurants, and entertainment facilities within the community.
