Grenland by Arloid Real Estate Development

Grenland is a coming-of-age modern and eclectic residential venture by the new and promising developer Arloid Real Estate Development in Dubai. Located in the heart of Dubai’s future ‘it’ destination i.e. Mohammed Bin Rashid City aka MBR City, the residential venture is certainly a promising one. Grenland comprises of finely structured, immaculately pre-furnished and pre-equipped 1BR, 2BR & 3BR apartments that come with a plethora of floor plans to choose from. All the houses have been fitted and furnished by some of the most renowned home furnishers globally and provide a thematic yet quite eclectic finish to the overall vibe of the houses. Project of a new developer with a fresh and unique approach to it, Grenland residences are a delight to reside in.

Premium Amenities

Special attention and profound consideration have been given to the provision of the finest set of facilities and amenities to the residents within these residences. The residential building comprises of a modern-gym pre-fitted with all the high-end equipment, an 18 meters long infinity pool for the residents to relish as well as special kids play area and pool too. Not just that, the residential development also comprises of spacious rooftop terraces featuring separate BBQ Zones and sun-beds for the residents to breathe and soak themselves in. In addition to all these exceptional facilities, the overall view of the development becomes all the more attractive and appealing with a 6 story high waterfall in the central courtyard of the building standing tall just at the entrance.

Convenient Location

In terms of its central location in MBR city, Grenland falls at very short distance to some key locations within the city i.e. 15 minute distance to Dubai Mall & Burj Khalifa and just a 20 minute distance to Dubai International Airport. Due to its proximal locations, Grenland also makes commotion feasible for the residents.
