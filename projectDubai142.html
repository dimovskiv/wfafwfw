Joya Verde Residences by Green Yard Properties

Based in the spacious and vibrant area of Jumeirah Village Circle in Dubai, Joya Verde Residences by Green Yard is quite obviously a country-themed residential complex comprising of studio, 1BR &2 BR apartments. Based in the stunning and magnificent community of Jumeirah Village Circle, the development’s infrastructure has been built and designed in an uber-contemporary manner to be a fit with its surroundings. It is certainly not a fact unknown in the region that Jumeirah Village Circle is indisputably one of the best things to happen to Dubai. Started off as an experimentation by the mega-developer Nakheel in the region, Jumeirah Village Circle’s founding idea was to amalgamate posh urban lifestyle in a village-like setting consisting of lush green gardens and free-flowing water canals. Thus, Jumeirah Village Circle proved out to be everything imagined and stands tall today as one of the most sought-after residential developments in the region due to its proximity to some of the busiest city districts and recreational spots as well as the tranquility and serenity it entails away from the mainstream hustle and bustle of this beaming metropolis. That is exactly the reason why Jumeirah Village Circle is chock-full with various Properties for Sale in Dubai within it be it the lavish Villas and Townhouses for Sale or commodiously structured and family-friendly apartments for Sale.

The Joya Verde Residences apartments are also all designed with immaculate intricacy and along the lines of a metropolitan lifestyle approach that seconds the very idea of the community this residential complex is based in.  Green Yard Properties is known to be a fairly new but surprisingly quite a trust-worthy name in the business for some years now and that is the very reason they have come up with another remarkable project in the shape of Joya Verde Residences serving as the pinnacle of comfortable lifestyle combined with unmatched finesse and intricacy in its overall design. Joya Verde Residences by Green Yard, as the name implies, is a green development with big foliages hanging out of the balconies of the homes within and one of the main attractions to this development is that the luxury houses are available at extremely affordable rates and easy payment plans. The Joya Verde Residence apartments are all designed with immaculate intricacy and along the lines of a metropolitan lifestyle approach that seconds the very idea of the community this residential complex is based in i.e. Jumeirah Village Circle. The interiors of the apartments are master-planned to be spacious and designed in a way that makes them commodious for the residents to embellish according to their own tastes and preferences.  Thus, it is certainly a major grab for families, couples, as well as singles alike since they can adorn, ornate and embellish their houses as per their own convenience and do not have to make do with a pre-designed house.

Premium Amenities

Joya Verde Residences by Green Yard is a residential community well-equipped with very quintessential amenity required by the residents. The development has a well-furnished health centre providing gym, yoga, as well as exercising space to the residents. Not just that, With lush green parks in the surroundings, the whole community bears a feel-good essence to it. Not to miss, there is a dedicated play area within the development for kids with the provision of carefully planned play-areas for children. In addition, all the apartments have access to the spacious swimming pool and tennis court. Thus, Joya Verde Residences do not only provide a facilitated neighborhood but also works towards providing them with a healthy atmosphere where they can play, exercise and spend time in much healthier activities. Not only that, but the development has a roof top garden to provide the residents with just the right amount of meditative nature proximity that they require. In addition, With absolutely free Podium level Parking spaces, no over-bearing charges and a very well-designed and facilitated neighborhood, Joya Verde Residences by Green Yard Properties lack nothing when it comes to superior provision of everything one requires within a residential community to live with.

Convenient Location

When choosing a dream house, one of the most important consideration for anybody is the convenience of location that place enjoys. Under this scenario, Joya Verde Residences enjoy a very feasible and nuclear location. They have been strategized and designed in such an order that they provide their residents with the ultimate ease of commute and so, they stand very true to their promise by doing exactly that. Joya Verde Residences by Green Yard is based in Jumeirah Village Circle and this very fact already makes it a very admirable, convenient and central location. The residential complex has access to all the travel touchpoints like metro links, cab stands, and bus stops. Based in the upbeat and vibrant Community of Jumeirah Village Circle, the development has direct access to important as well as recreational places like  a 15 minute distance to Mall of the Emirates and Dubai Marina, and just around a 20 minute distance to Downtown Dubai, Dubai Mall & Burj Khalifa as well as some of the other major key locations in the city making the inclusion of recreational, leisure as well as necessary facilities a surety in their residents’ lives. However, it does not stop there. The master developers of this stunning residential venture i.e. Green Yard Properties also aim and plan to build two of the most proficient malls within the proximity of their residential development to provide the residents with literally one-step away convenience. The Circle Mall will be a 1 million sq ft wide mall with around 200 shops which would be retail, leisure and recreational in their nature. The mall will also have an extended food court and is strategically planned to be placed between Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road, Al Khail Road, and Hessa Street. This mall will serve the residents with the utmost ease and feasibility. Then there would be Al Khail Avenue, a large street avenue covering an area of around 3.6 million sq ft of retail spaces, with several leisure and recreational facilities like diners, restaurants and cafes, shops and outlets, cinemas would be the epitome of the perfect place to hang out within the vicinity. Al Khail Avenue is aimed to be gigantic in its area and would be made to accommodate multi-storey car park services within it.
