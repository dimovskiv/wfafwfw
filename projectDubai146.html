Rahaal Building 5 by Dubai Holding

Rahaal is Dubai Holding‘s most recent and much-anticipated freehold residential addition to their current path-breaking establishment of Madinat Jumeirah Living. Rahaal marks as the 4th residential venture of Dubai Holding in the stunning vicinity of Madinat Jumeirah, more popularly known as MJ within the region. The introduction and launch of this 4th residential building is the testimony in itself to the fact that all the residential ventures within the area are already packed and are in great demand because of the unmatched infrastructure and facilities that they entail. Situated along the tract of Jumeirah, this luxury residential development can be expected to be all things glitz, chic and classy. Rahaal has been positioned and marketed by Dubai Holding as the Ride of the Lifetime offering a larger than life exposure and vibes to the residents.

Rahaal’s majestic apartments with high-end finishes make for exceptional relaxed living. These 1BR, 2BR, 3BR & 4BR apartments are intricately designed and meticulously furnished with everything unrivaled and unique living up to the promise that Madinat Jumeirah entails in each one of its offerings. The whole residential vicinity features air-conditioned walkways keeping the overall temperature of the neighborhood optimum at all times and giving the residents an ultimate shield and protection from harsh climatic conditions and temperatures. The whole experience of exquisiteness starts from the very first moment one steps inside the lobby of this lavish building. The lobby lounges are designed in such tranquilizing and calming notes that it induces a whole new experience of warmth for the residents. Rahaal in certainly nothing less than a visual treat with very calm and serene undertones and interior which are quite refreshing yet calming to the residents’ eyes. The whole layout of the residential setting in very well laid out, planned and executed. Therefore, it would not be wrong to say that Rahaal in Madinat Jumeirah is the epitome of elite living within the most desirable convenient location with the finest set of facilities and is only expected to escalate more and more in its demand and value.

Convenient Location

Rahaal is situated in the luxury freehold residential development of Madinat Jumeirah lying opposite to the iconic Burj Al Arab in the vivacious and vibrant neighborhood of Palm Jumeirah. Burj Al Arab is considered to be an icon of luxury and before the iconic skyscraper of Burj Khalifa came into the scene, Burj Al Arab was it location of the region and therefore, it still holds the same grandeur and popularity within the region. Its central location provides ease of connectivity through well-established road networks to the rest of the city: Sheikh Zayed Road, Jumeirah Road, Al Wasl Road, Umm Suqeim road all within just a 5-minute drive. It is also adjacent to major shopping malls, public transport links, and schools at its doorstep.

At just a 2 minute drive from Sheikh Zayed Road, 5 minute drive from Dubai Media City, 8 minute drive from Dubai Internet City, and a very convenient distance from Dubai International Airport, Rahaal at Madinat Jumeirah certainly enjoys a very central location when it comes to its accessibility and reach towards other key locations of the city. Also, since the whole development of Madinat Jumeirah by Dubai Holding in centered in such a commercially popular area, it is only expected to have increased demand in the future with more and more local as well as global investors investing their hard earned money into this mega-project.
