Resortz by Danube Properties

Resortz by Danube is a luxurious apartment project located in the magnificent community of Arjan, next to Miracle Gardens (along Umm Suqeim Road & Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road). It has a plot area of around 4 hectares and its nearest community center is the newly-opened Al Barsha My City Centre. Resortz spreads over 4 acres of land area in Dubai and is organized over 3 blocks.

Resortz by Danube boasts of a beautiful dome in the central block that is reminiscent of the Duomo in Florence. There is also a detailed Column that stands tall throughout the facade of the buildings adding to its grandeur. With its location being very close to Miracle Garden, residents are sure to enjoy living the urban life in a resort style.
