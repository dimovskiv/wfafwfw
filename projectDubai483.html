1/JBR by Dubai Properties

1 JBR is a luxury apartment project by Dubai Properties located in the waterfront community of Jumeirah Beach Residence. This astonishing residential project boasts of full sea views, as well as views of Palm Jumeirah and the Blue Waters Island which can be can be viewed through the homes’ glass facades. The stunning 1 JBR project features two, three and four-bedroom apartments and 5-bedroom penthouses with stunning interiors that make you savor the comfort of home. Space will not be a problem, as each floor will only have three to four residences while to the topmost part will be reserved for penthouses. All the residences are designed with A-Grade high-end finishes.

One JBR Full Sea Views

Premium Amenities

1 JBR features all amenities that promises convenience and the ultimate luxury lifestyle. The project has semi-private elevator lobbies until the 30th floor and a private lift lobby for each penthouse at the parking level. The residences are designed with a posh finishes and designer fittings, with every living room boasting of eye-catching panoramas. Each unit is equipped with smart home technology and a private pool that also showcase wonderful views while you swim. Residents also have the option to customize their apartments according to their preferences. Enjoy working out on its high-standard gym with a beachfront view, access VIP parking spaces. 1 JBR even offers direct beach access straight from your home.

Convenient Location

1 JBR is located in the most sought after neighborhood of Jumeirah Beach Residence. The area is not only known as a tourist hotspot, but also an ideal residential neighborhood bustling with activity. The area is home to several residential and commercial developments and is also home to Dubai’s most popular hotels and resorts.  Step out and stroll around the world-class hotels, boutiques, restaurants and coffee shops in Jumeirah Beach Residence.
