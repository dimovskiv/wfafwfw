Asayel Building 1 by Dubai Holding

Asayel, one of the newer projects of Madinat Jumeirah Living, is set to make its arrival very soon. Located in one of the more popular localities in Dubai, Asayel can be found within the Um Suqeim community, located in Western Dubai, with fantastic views of the Burj Al Arab. Since Asayel is positioned along Jumeirah Beach, this property is classified as prime and exclusive as Jumeirah is home to large properties, some of which will simply take your breath away.

Asayel: Where the Past Meets the Present

In addition to that, Asayel is known to be one of the latest apartments being built in the area. Surely, it does not disappoint as it offers premium living at its finest whilst retaining the charm of Dubai through its architectural design. In fact, this is the place where the past meets the present. With a design that will surely remind you of home accompanied by a lifestyle that is definitely top notch, there is not one person that this place cannot bring happiness to.

Asayel: All for One, One for All

For growing families, this place is ideal as it meets both the expectations of the parents or the older members of the family as well as the children or younger members of the family. Since Asayel is located within the reach of Jumeirah beach, you can expect to have your private little getaway and have the waterfront views every single day. Which such a picturesque sight, this should help the adults clear their mind and allow for the children to have some fun as well.

Asayel: Work and Play

Asayel is connected to the Sheikh Zayed  Road which is only a two (2) minute drive away. As for working areas such as Dubai Media City and Dubai Internet City, you can get there in less than ten (10) minutes. If you find that you will need to travel often for work, this place is best as Dubai International Airport is only twenty-five (25) minutes away from your home — a definite win as you cut down on travel time quite a bit.

Asayel: Keeping It Comfortable

 With wide spaces, state-of-the-art gyms, exclusive retail areas, air-conditioned walkways, and people that you can build meaningful relationships with, Asayel will definitely enhance your life for the better. With a flexible payment plan, Asayel combines luxury with classic beauty and by taking part in this exceptional development you can never go wrong – and at this point, you know it too.
