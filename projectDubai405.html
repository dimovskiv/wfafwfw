Ruby Apartments by Deyaar Properties

Ruby is a low-rise residential apartment tower located in the unique and fully-integrated neighborhood of Dubai Silicon Oasis (DSO). The 8-storey building offers a total of 88 apartments available in spacious studio units, 1-bedroom, and 2-bedroom apartments.

The elegant residences by Dubai-based developer Deyaar are located in close proximity to the Dubai Outsource Zone (DOZ). Ruby apartments are also near Sheikh Zayed University and other renowned institutions in its Educational Zone. Residents can also easily access high-end amenities with the area such as its sky leisure deck, swimming pool, garden, and BBQ area. There is also a fully-equipped gymnasium, multi-purpose hall, basement and surface car parking, CCTV and access control, 24-hour security system, fire alarm, fire fighting system, and high-speed internet connectivity.

Surrounding the area is a selection of restaurants, a shopping mall and cafes for your leisure convenience. There is also a community center and other recreational destinations seen to arise within the neighborhood.
