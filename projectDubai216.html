Five Jumeirah Village Dubai by Five Holdings

Five Jumeirah Village Dubai by FIVE Holdings is a luxury hotel project located in the fantastic Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC) community. The five-star tower will be featuring 247 hotel rooms and suites, available in 221 one-bedroom and two-bedroom hotel apartments and 33 four-bedroom hotel apartments.

The luxury hospitality tower has been an iconic project and has received numerous awards including Leisure and Hospitality Project of the Year 2015 at the Middle East Architect Awards; the Tall Buildings category at the 2016 Architectural Review; and the Best International, New Hotel Construction & Design & Hotel Architecture at the 2016 International Property Awards. The fantastic design of the property, which was conceptualized by the in-house design studio of FIVE Holdings, has been patented and registered as a trademark in 168 countries across the world.

Premium Amenities

Five Jumeirah Village Dubai features all premium amenities, including private pools in each of the hotel units. Enjoy its wealth of podium-level amenities, which will feature a fully-equipped gym, boutique spa, swimming pool, and a strip of restaurants. Stroll along its green walkways and admire its refreshing water features, a perfect place to relax and chill after a long and tiring day. Convenient Location

Five Jumeirah Village Dubai is strategically positioned in the renowned neighborhood of Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC). Linked to major road networks, the hospitality project provides easy access to different parts of Dubai. The area is also just a short drive away from the Al Maktoum International Airport and the EXPO 2020 site. The community is designed for residential living with a selection villa, townhouses, and apartments filled with landscaped parks and outdoor activity areas. Five Jumeirah Village Dubai will be at the forefront of a plethora of leisure, shopping, and entertainment destinations featuring department stores, supermarkets, multi-screen cinema, health clinics, cafes, restaurants, and a food court.
