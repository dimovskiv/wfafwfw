Al Furjan Townhouses by Nakheel Properties

An epitome of modern urban housing, Al Furjan is a residential venture by Al Nakheel comprising of immaculately spacious 3 BR townhouses. Al Furjan imparts a quintessential Arabic vibe through its infrastructure and fine internal furnishing. The 3BR townhouses are categorized and available in two types. i.e. The 3BR Townhouse Middle and The 3BR Townhouse Corner. Quite self-explanatory, Al Furjan gives its buyers full autonomy and freedom of choice based on their preferences whichever direction they want to choose from. Regardless of the placement, all the key dimensional features i.e. Area in sq.m, terraces, plot size, interior of the house etc. are all exactly the same for both types.

Al Furjan Villas and Townhouses

Premium Amenities

Al Furjan possesses all the posh amenities to it. The development has dedicated community centers namely Al Furjan Pavillion and Al Furjan West Pavillion that home these exceptional amenities to them i.e. Swimming pools, sports courts, cafes, gym, mosques, restaurants as well as retail facilities.

Al Furjan Villas & Townhouses – Interior

Convenient Location

Location serves as one of the favorable parts to Al Furjan with its close proximity to the recreational hub of Dubai i.e. Sheikh Zayed Road. The development is also closely linked to Sheikh Muhammad Bin Zayed Road and Al Yalayis Road. Such ease of access to some of Dubai’s best roads makes Al Furjan a very fastly growing and popular community to reside in.
