Bvlgari Marina Lofts by Meraas Holding

BVLGARI Marina Lofts by Meraas Holding features a collection of homes located in Jumeira Bay. The sophistication of an open space concept nestled at a high-end maritime complex makes the BVLGARI Marina Lofts one of the most exclusive residences in Dubai. Floor-to-ceiling windows allow natural light to spread expansively into the unit, while also offering magnificent marina views. The lofts showcase contemporary design, meticulous craftsmanship, and artistic detail.

Ranging from three to four-bedroom, the spectacular living spaces are accompanied by a private lawn overlooking the spectacular marina. Each home at the BVLGARI Residences has been meticulously designed to create efficient living spaces. A luminous living room, dining room, and kitchen add a distinct character to space as well. BVLGARI’s signature style is evident in every detail whether inside the home, in the common areas or within the wider community.

Amenities and Facilities

Access state-of-the-art and world-class amenities within the building including several swimming pools, covered parking spaces, private beach club, large gymnasium and residence meeting facilities. Also, neighboring the area of BVLGARI Marina Lofts are a choice of shops, food and beverage outlets, and cafes.

At the BVLGARI Marina Lofts, individual landscaped terraces provide relaxing outdoor areas while also maximizing privacy from the promenade. The fresh colour palette and high-quality materials are an embodiment of the exclusive location blend seamlessly with the adjacent residences as well. Each unit features a staircase as well as a lift and have two parking bays within the private carport at the entrance.

Convenient Location

BVLGARI Marina Lofts are situated at the most prestigious location of Dubai “Jumeirah Bay”. In fact, it is the perfect combination of mesmerizing views, world-class amenities, and connectivity to Dubai’s best landmarks. Also, this is a place where you will experience the true meaning of wellness. This is simply because you will live the royal style. As well as these, this BVLGARI Marina Lofts will be your magnificent mansion at the heart of a middle-eastern paradise.

Nearby Places

In addition, the residents of  BVLGARI Marina Lofts can enjoy a host of dining options and wellness facilities within the resort, while benefiting from the unmatched service standards of the BVLGARI Resort. The pedestrian-only promenade connects the BVLGARI hotel building with the Yacht Club. Inspired by Italian seaside harbours, it hosts world-famous restaurants and cafes, providing the finest culinary experiences. The Marina flanks the 46-boat harbour whose travertine promenade is illuminated by street lamps at dusk, making it the ultimate yachting address with berths ranging from 10 to 40 meters.
