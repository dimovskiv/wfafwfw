Azizi Mirage 1 by Azizi Developments

Azizi Mirage 1 is a modern residential project by Azizi Developments located in Dubai Studio City. The building’s evident modern and futuristic design is like a merging of two towers into one singular L-shaped structure within a cutting-edge framed design. With a total built-up area of 38,162.30 square meters, Azizi Mirage 1 features a 2 basement, a ground level podium and eight floors for its 444 residences. The homes are available in 253 studio, 155 one-bedroom, and 36 two-bedroom units.

Premium Amenities

The variety of layouts of Azizi Mirage 1 guarantees smart use of space and give you the liberty to customize your own apartment. Its open-plan layout gives you more room to experiment, while its wide windows allow you to admire nature-inspired views. The residences are designed with a chic and modern feel, with elegant lighting fixtures and integrated paneling elements. Each bedroom is designed with built-in wardrobes, mirrors, and vanity units. You can enjoy the privacy of your bedroom while enjoying natural sunlight in your floor-to-ceiling windows. The kitchens, meantime, are fully furnished with premium appliances, complementing the overall design of its sophisticated countertops and dining area. The bathrooms are designed with high-standard materials from tiling to fittings all ensuring sophistication and elegance.

Convenient Location

Nestled in the heart of Dubai’s premier locations, the Azizi Mirage 1 in Dubai Studio City has located next to the EXPO 2020 venue. The area also provides convenient access to major road networks including the Al Hessa and Al Qudra Roads which allows you to travel in major destinations in Dubai including the DWC airport and the City Centre Me’aisem, in just a few minutes. The community of Dubai Studio City is home of production hubs and sound stages, making you at the center of modern entertainment and shows.
