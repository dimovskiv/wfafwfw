Green Diamond One by Diamond Developers

Built within the master community of Arjan, Green Diamond one is a beautiful, nature- friendly, ecologically considerate one-of-a-kind residential development. From the luscious green foliages hanging out from the balconies of the apartments to the front line solar panel planted within the vicinity, the place is a true natural habitat. Green Diamond One located in Arjan, Dubai are two commodious residential twin structures both consisting of 8 storeys each.  With a total of 194 beautiful houses, the buildings are built along the lines of automated environmental sustainability approach. All the houses are fitted with LED lighting to check excessive energy utilization. In addition, all the apartments are pre-fitted with the latest high-end water-saving apparatuses that help conserve clean water for the occupants. Offering 2BR and 3BR apartments with easy installment payment plans, Green Diamond One is a definite no-miss!

Green Diamond One Apartments

PREMIUM AMENITIES

As explained earlier, the community itself is a delightful, full of nature, and vibrant one as opposed to a conventional ‘no-green-all- sand’  type which is a major grab for people who love and enjoy an earthy country-side feel. But, as if it was not enough, the place owns a multi-purpose deck area, a temperature-monitored huge swimming pool, a fully equipped gymnasium and of course, an extremely colorful and interactively built children’s play area.

Green Diamond One – Amenitie

CONVENIENT LOCATION

Due to its location in Arjan, Dubailand, the residential development enjoys both a recreational proximity to tourist attractions like Dubai Miracle Garden as well as access to some vital lifestyle locations such as hospitals, schools, metro links, and bus stops too. Healthcare facilities like Saudi German Hospital, Al Zahra Hospital, and Aster Medical Centre are situated close to the community. In addition to it, schools such as GEMS, JSS International are some of very closely situated schools to Green Diamond One. As the community has huge magnitude, metro links from three different sides are connected to it. i.e. Mall of the Emirates metro link, Sharaf DG metro, and Dubai Internet City metro link fall as the three closest commotional touch points to the development.
