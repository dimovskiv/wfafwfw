Amaranta 4 by Dubai Properties

An epitome of modern and urban lifestyle, Amaranta 4 as a feather is one of the latest additions to the already over cluttered hat of achievements by Dubai Properties. This residential venture, as its name implies, is all things beautiful, tranquilizing, stunning and serene. Comprising of spacious thematic 3BR and 4BR townhouses, Amaranta 4 by Dubai Properties is the ultimate haven and habitat for a facilitated living. All these 3BR and 4BR townhouses are fully furnished and follow an eclectic theme that further adds to their elegance and the whole feel of the house. All the houses are built and constructed keeping residents’ ease and convenience in mind. A project of Dubai Properties, the residential development strictly cohorts to sustainable living and has been designed to follow the same patterns too.

Premium Amenities

Amaranta 4 markets this residential development as the mediator between fitness and fun because it provides a plethora of options for both. Be it its state-of-the-art gymnasium, the swimming pool cycling tracks or walking pathways, Dubai Properties has got you covered with all the superior lifestyle amenities. In addition to all these, the insertion of large open spaces, BBQ areas, comfort lounges in the vicinity add further sentiment to the whole place.

Convenient Location

Amaranta 4 by Dubai Properties is situated in Villanova. It is due to this central location, it enjoys close proximity to some of the major locations in the city. The whole townhouse residential development lies at a junction between Al Qudra Road, Al Ain Road & Emirates Road. This central location makes it conveniently accessible to some key locations in the city like Downtown Dubai, Dubai Marina, and The Beach etc at very accessible distances.
