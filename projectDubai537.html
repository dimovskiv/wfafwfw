Creek Horizon by Emaar Properties

Creek Horizon is located adjacent to the central park of the Island District at Dubai Creek Harbour. Its towers offer remarkable vistas from each of over 40 storeys, soaring elegantly over verdant parks and sparkling waterways. With over 500 contemporary apartments of various sizes, Creek Horizon offers elevated experiences of exceptional style. Creek Horizon’s two sleek towers take stylish modern living to new heights. The residences enjoy the prime position over the district’s marina, perfectly framing Burj Khalifa and Downtown Dubai on the horizon. The prime apartments offer the finest facilities and a wealth of attractive amenities to craft a lifestyle of extraordinary comfort and convenience.
