Janayen Avenue by Dubai Investments Real Estate

Janayen Avenue is a unique residential project by Dubai Investments Real Estate Company located in the Mirdif Hills development. The concept of these residences is ‘vertical villas’ inspired to bring the benefits of both high rise and traditional house living. This idea came from the theory of French architect ‘Le Corbusier’ who has the vision to create better-living spaces in populated cities. The premium apartments in the Mirdif Hills location are available in one, two and three bedroom apartments and four-bedroom duplexes.

Premium Amenities

Janayen Avenue welcomes you to a stunning lobby with astonishing features. The living spaces are designed with an ultra modern living room, a comfortable bedroom, and an elegant bathroom. Integrated into the residences’ landscape are greenery and plants on every floor, all adhering to the developer’s vision to promote sustainable and ecological projects. This Mirdif Hills project will be featuring a children’s pool, an outdoor jacuzzi, and a fully-equipped gymnasium. A kids’ play area and recreational facilities will be easily accessible in a fully-secured building. The area will also provide retail shops, a multi-purpose hall and a community center.

Janayen Avenue – Interiors

Convenient Location

Janayen Avenue is situated in the prestigious Mirdif Hills development, a residential community with the Dubai skyline as its backdrop. Janayen Avenue is just one of the three residential districts which include Nasayem Avenue and Al Multaqa Avenue. Aside from its residential districts, the mixed-use residential neighborhood features commercial, residential, hotel and retail development which spans up to 3.9 million square feet. Mirdif Hills apartments and Mirdif Hills villas perfectly blend with the old and new Dubai, by keeping its heritage evident in its architecture and culture. Enjoy all the leisure, dining and entertainment options within the area, making shopping, playing,  and doing business all within your reach. The premier hospital will also be developed in the area, all equipped with state-of-the-art equipment with the service of its international staff.
