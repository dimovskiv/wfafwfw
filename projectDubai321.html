O Ten Apartments by Aqua Properties

O Ten Apartments by Aqua Properties is a ten-storey residential project located in Dubai Healthcare City (DHCC) offering studio, one and two bedroom arrangements. These luxury homes are situated in a gated community, ensuring security and safety for its residents.

The premium apartments feature two floors of basement parking for visitors and a range of retail spaces on the ground floor. O Ten offers the finest amenities you would surely love including an infinity pool with wooden deck lounge areas, interlocking walkways amid landscaping waterfalls and an advanced gym that features interesting views.
