The Sterling by Omniyat

The Sterling is a luxury residential project consisting of two stunning towers situated in the Burj Khalifa District in Downtown Dubai. Designed by premier property developer Omniyat, the two majestic towers are connected by halls that make up the lobby, retail areas, and elevated gardens. With intelligently-designed interiors and outstanding architectural facade, The Sterling has become one of the iconic towers that glow in Downtown at night.

The Sterling – Elegant Apartments

The Sterling features 25-storeys which house a selection of studio, 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, and 3-bedroom apartments, exclusive townhouses and elegant penthouses all showcasing impressive views of the city. Both towers boast of amazing views of the Burj Khalifa, Mohammed bin Rashid City, and The Creek.

Premium Amenities

The Sterling is designed with a distinct grandeur of its own, combining both minimalism and warmth. It lobby is faced with beautiful chrome finish, while each of its apartment units are equipped with designer furniture. The residences are crafted with high-quality materials and with windows that all allow the abundance of natural light. With residents given controlled access and full concierge services, you can be sure that convenience is given utmost priority in The Sterling. Residents can control exposure to daylight, especially during summer to conserve cooling energy inside the home. Plus, discover the finest amenities that will exceed your expectations including lounge areas, state-of-the-art gymnasium, swimming pools, and spa.

The Sterling – Lobby

Convenient Location

The Sterling Apartments and Penthouses are located in the vibrant neighborhood of Downtown Dubai. The residences are located less than 7 kilometers away from Dubai International Financial Center (DIFC), 11 kilometers away from Trade Center, and 10 minutes away from the future Dubai Design District. Easily access the biggest mall in the world, The Dubai Mall, or shop at exclusive designer fashion brands like Chanel, Dior and Louis Vuitton to name a few. Being located near the Al Khail Road, you can easily reach the different destinations in Dubai.
