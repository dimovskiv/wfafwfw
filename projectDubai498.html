Glitz Residence 3 by Danube Properties

Glitz Residence 3 is designed to ensure that every resident gets ultimate lifestyle comforts and contemporary convenience. The unique residences by Danube Properties offer a selection of 352 units available in studio, 1, 2 and 3-bedroom contemporary styled apartments and 8 retail outlets to cater to the resident’s needs. Glitz Residence 3 aims to set a benchmark in providing the perfect environment for community living.

Every detail of the stylish abodes complements the lifestyle expected by today’s young urban family, including the green landscaped environment location. Glitz Residence 3 combines spacious living spaces for the family with the right balance of privacy and outdoor living.

Amenities

  • Lavishly designed Grand Lobby with a water theme.
  • Greenery laden leisure deck with Barbecue station.
  • Lift lobby with welcoming flower box on each level.
  • Rooftop Mini Golf.
  • Two landscaped gardens with an open Air Gazebo at the Podium Level.
  • Air Conditioned Kids play area and party hall.
  • Health Club with Jacuzzi, Steam room &amp; Sauna.
  • Rooftop swimming pool.
  • The ground level will feature Retail, F&amp;B and a convenience store.

Location
