Jewels Apartments by Danube Properties

Jewelz Apartments by Danube is a selection of luxury furnished apartments in the vibrant community of Arjan. The residential project offers high-class living spaces with comfortable features. The elegant building is aesthetically designed to blend with its surroundings. Jewelz Apartments has 463 freehold residences are available in 311 studios, 104 one bedroom, and 48 two-bedroom units. The ground floor of the tower features 2 podiums reserved for parking spaces.

The homes offer a very flexible payment plan with up to 5 years post-handover payment (1% per month in 54 months). Jewelz Apartments by Danube also offers up to 15% return of investment.

Premium Amenities

Jewelz by Danube offers furnished apartment units with unique features including a living room that can be converted into a bedroom at night. Danube ensures that this feature is equipped with activated sensors making it safe even for kids. The living spaces feature carefully designed furniture and appliances, minimalist finishes, glossy ceramic tiles, and Italian bath fittings. Jewelz provides a wide range of premium amenities within the elegant tower and even in its surrounding community. Step into its grand entrance lobby designed with high-class finishes and choice materials. Its lift lobby features elaborate moldings, stylish flooring and elegant furnishings that will surely leave you impressed. Admire its landscaped garden with refreshing water features on the podium level, and its air-conditioned multipurpose hall that also doubles as a children’s play area.

Achieve your fitness goals with its state-of-the-art health club and fully-equipped gymnasium with steam room and sauna. Jewelz Apartments provide a nice swimming pool, kids’ pool, and jacuzzi. There is also a dedicated barbeque deck within the building. Jewelz Apartments will provide well-lit covered parking area and a surveillance system access control.

Convenient Location

Jewelz by Danube is located in the freehold and mixed-use community of Arjan in Dubailand. The development which will be comprised of commercial towers, residences complexes, retail, and hospitality developments. An open-air amphitheater will also be built within the area, which will be a future venue of major events in Dubai. The six-themed district is seen to be a future tourist attraction with cultural destinations and premium recreational facilities. Arjan is located just opposite Motor City and off the Umm Suqueim Road, next to the Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Road. Easily access major destinations in Dubai including several recreational parks and sporting areas.
