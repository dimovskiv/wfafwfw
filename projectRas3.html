Bayti Residences by Al Hamra Real Estate Developers

Bayti in Arabic means 'my home'. Hence, Bayti Residences are built and designed in the same spirit too. The luxurious urban lifestyle take on this residential project is what makes it unique as well as desirable at the same time. Bayti is a residential project flourishing in the backdrop of Al Hamra Village- a project of a renowned group i.e. Al Hamra Real Estate Developers , consisting of a cluster of several townhouses. Comprising of 3BR Villas and 4BR Villas, these townhouses are triple-storey structures with well-secured roofs and terraces. The interiors of the townhouses curate foyers, staircases, pavements etc. Bayti currently stands firm as one of the most luxurious looking precedent of the Gulf family culture in the UAE. Not only that, its thoughtfulness with regards to sustainable living is what makes it unique too. Bayti homes are designed in such a way that they make use of the latest sustainability techniques to reduce unnecessary consumption. All homes are installed with solar heated and energy efficient water-tanks, low-flow water fixtures, CO detectors on each floor, low VOC- paints and LED lightning to reduce adverse effects on the natural environment. Having said that, it would be safe to say that Bayti is an ideal value for money and property, it is not a house but already a home.

Bayti Residences Premium Amenities

The Ritz Calton, Hilton, and Waldorf Astoria are few of the globally renowned restaurant options that lie in the surroundings of Bayti at Al Hamra Village. In addition to this, it is a few minute walk from the townhouses to immaculate Marina and Private Beach. Not to miss, the place is covered on all three sides by large sustainable recreational parks. But the most important aspect while selecting a home is its proximity to schools and offices for the family members. The place is just at a walking distance from RAK Academy School and is connected to some commercial offices in the near-about too. Thus, be it kids or adults, Bayti serves as a perfect fit to all.
