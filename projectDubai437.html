Anwa Residences by Omniyat

Anwa Residences is an apartment tower situated in heart of Dubai Maritime City. The portrait of ANWA features the endless views of the ocean, with the shining tower raised as a landmark in the emirate. The residences symbolize the former glory of the magnificent grand Transatlantic cruise voyages in the 20’s century.

The 48-storey tower is consist of a variety of arrangements ranging from studios, one to three-bedroom apartments, penthouses and townhouses which feature high-standard amenities including, gymnasium, hotel & spa facilities, infinity pool, and sports facilities.
