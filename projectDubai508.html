Ashjar by Al Barari Developers

Ashjar in Al Barari is a selection of contemporary apartments housed in nine low-rise and low-density buildings. The residences which come in 1, 2 and 3-bedroom apartments, sized at 1,700 to 4,900 square feet. With waterways and greenery surrounding the residences, Ashjar in Al Barari is your ideal home in Dubai. The homes are available in three designs that reflect its unique exteriors including Extrovert, Introvert and Cube. Premium Amenities

Ashjar in Al Barari features chic and modern homes, primarily using white as its primary color. The residences are designed in a way that makes it an intimate and private layout, but with shared facilities such as an underground parking space, an infinity-edge pool, a kids’ playground, and the popular landscaped gardens. Enjoy high-speed internet access and smart home technology all ensuring your convenience. The residences also provide a driver’s room, and access to reception, cleaning and laundry services. Other facilities accessible for the residents are its barbeque area, steam room, state-of-the-art gym and its sport s court areas.

Convenient Location

Ashjar is nestled strategically in the Al Barari community, a green neighborhood that combines modern and nature-inspired living. The secured and gated location is an ideal haven that allows both privacy and connectivity, being linked to major road networks and with future plans for metro lines. Al Barari is just a 15-minute drive away from the city and is surrounded by necessity areas such as retail outlets, a business school, several medical facilities, and a six-star resort hotel. The area is also home to a wide range of residential projects all ensuring the Al Barari lifestyle. With 60% of the development dedicated to rich forestry and lush green landscapes, you will truly find your dream residence in Ashjar in Al Barari.
