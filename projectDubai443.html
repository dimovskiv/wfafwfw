Miraclz Tower by Danube Properties

Miraclz Tower by Danube is a ground-breaking residential concept that sets an excellent standard for your home. The apartment tower is located in Arjan next to the tourist destination, Miracle Gardens. Miraclz Tower hosts 591 apartments ranging from of Studio Apartments, 1-Bedroom Apartments and 2-Bedrooms Apartments, with sizes from 390 square ft. to 1184 square ft. The residences are fully-furnished, each fitted with modular furniture and a European technology convertible sofa that transforms into a full-sized bed at a gentle pull.
