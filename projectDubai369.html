Eaton Place by Ellington Properties

Eaton Place is a modern apartment project by Ellington Properties offering studio, one-bedroom, and two-bedroom apartments in the opulent community of Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC). The apartments are specially designed to cater to individuals and families belonging to the middle-class segment with its affordable yet elegant homes. Eaton Place offers beautifully-designed interiors creating a warm and serene ambiance, with carefully-chosen hues playing on neutrals and pastel colors.
