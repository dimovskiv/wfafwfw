The Opus Commercial Tower by Omniyat

The Opus is a spectacular commercial tower designed within the business capital across UAE.  The Opus Commercial Tower is located just in Business Bay, designed by the award-winning architect Zaha Hadid. The amazing design of a glowing cube in the midst of The Opus Tower is just one of the unique features of the creatively-designed architectural masterpiece. The tower also boasts of awe-inspiring views of the Burj Dubai area, the Dancing Tower, the Dubai Financial Market, and amazing waterfront views.

The Opus Residence

The Opus provides a revolutionary concept in offices with AAA-class and an efficiency rating of 87%, combining the best of form and functionality unlike anywhere else in Dubai. The Opus Commercial Tower offices come with a one to three years payment plan and offer exclusive discounts for those who will pay in cash.

Premium Amenities

With its expensive frontage appearance, The Opus provides 21-storeys consisting of one large podium plus three floors dedicated for hotels. The floating cube can catch anyone’s attention during the daytime, and the stunning lightning design of the tower will express the brightness during night time. This innovative design has been known as one of the benchmark designs in architecture not just in Dubai but all over the world.

The Opus Residence – Landscaping

The highest floor which is located above the 50-meter bridge connected between the two towers, featuring innovative amenities including a ‘Tranquility Zone’, a beach deck with reflective pool, shaded roof terrace, media zone, and a gym.

Each of The Opus Towers (both the residential and commercial) will have its own lobby, but all towers will be serviced by one concentrated core. Service lifts will be provided as well communal restrooms an pantry facilities. Its elaborate retail podium which will occupy the ground, first and second floor will feature fashion boutiques, F&B venues, cafes, and restaurants.

Convenient Location

The vibrant and modern commercial hub of Business Bay is the location of The Opus Commercial Tower. This area has been designed to become Dubai’s business capital, a strategic area for regional and international headquarters of large corporations and multi-national companies. Enjoy easy connectivity to various part of Dubai as The Opus Commercial Tower in Business Bay is just situated off the commercial corridor of Sheikh Zayed Road, between interchanges 1 and 2. Although displaying the modern metropolis ambience, The Opus Commercial Tower will be nestled among landscaped gardens and road networks and waterways.
