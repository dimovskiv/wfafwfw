Farhad Azizi Residence by Azizi Developments

Farhad Azizi Residence is a high-end residential apartment building located in Dubai Healthcare City, The premier residential project by Azizi Developments features 634 uniquely-designed luxurious apartments which boast of quality facilities and amenities. Each home in Farhad Azizi Residence is delivered with fresh and modern interior design, allowing residents to live in absolute comfort and style. Enjoy fantastic views of Burj Khalifa and the foreseen tallest tower in the world, the Dubai Creek Tower.

Farhad Azizi Residence is also strategically nestled within the heart of the healthcare district of Dubai, situated in close proximity to Downtown Dubai, Dubai Airport, Al Khor and several high-end malls. The neighborhood is also best connected to a metro station and has convenient access to major roads like the Al Khail Road and Oud Metha Road. Prices for its studio apartments start at AED 510,000.
