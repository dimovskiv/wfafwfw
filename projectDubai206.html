Jumeirah Island Townhouses by Nakheel Properties

A town-house residential community located in close vicinage to Al Khail and Sheikh Zayed Road, Jumeirah Islands Townhouses; more popularly known as (JIT) is a beautiful development of 4BR townhouses fenced by a gated community. The townhouses are designed, built, and furnished with a quintessential priority order to open space and a feel-good ambience around the house. Each house owns a dedicated and commodious garden area and veranda on the ground floor. The wooden doors in the houses instill a historic touch to the whole interior accompanied by crystal clear porcelain floors serenading the residents to a state of being in their comfort zone. Adorned by some of the best interior designers in the market, based on the residential community’s location, the interiors of the house come with an islandic vibes to them e.g. the steps and stairways are made using exotic aquatic stones. Thus, it would be safe to say that this residential project is all things aquatic and serenity.

Jumeirah Island Townhouses – Bedroom

Premium Amenities

Shadowed on all four sides with lush green fields, the exceptional amenities i.e. a large communal swimming pool, waterfront view, inside restaurants and retail outlets make life all the more easier and enjoyable for the residents residing in this beautiful development called Jumeirah Islands Townhouses (JIT). However, based on an Island, there had to be something uniquely distinct about this residential community which is that all the townhouses face beautiful waterfront views and have access to Jumeirah Island Pavilion. Jumeirah Island Pavilion is a waterfront leisure hub that engulfs a whole dose of retreat for the residents in the form of restaurants, shopping areas and retail shops.

Jumeirah Island Townhouses – Amenities
