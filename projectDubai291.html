Al Mamzar District Plot by Meraas Holding

Al Mamzar District Plot is a freehold residential villa plot located on Cairo Road, close to Al Ittihad Private School. The plot is just a few minutes away from the Al Mamzar open beach.

The permitted height for villas in Al Mamzar District Plot is G+1. The plot is available for cash payment on a payment plan for 3 years. There is no time limit for the construction of the villas.

Price for Al Mamzar District Plot is confidential and will be revealed to interested buyers only.

Details

  • Permitted Height: G+1
  • Usage: Residential Villa
  • Ownership: Freehold
  • No time limit to construct
  • No service charges
  • Early Access is provided upon covering 60% of payment
