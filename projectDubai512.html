The Floating Seahorse Villas by The Heart of Europe

The Floating Seahorse Villas is a truly unique underwater product which is the first of its kind in the world. It is an exclusive marine style retreat (essentially a boat without propulsion) which has its own plot in the Arabian Gulf. It is an iconic and one of a kind luxury lifestyle product which was officially unveiled in the year 2015 of March at the Dubai International Boat Show. With four flexi-living and sleeping areas, The Signature Edition of The Floating Seahorse Villas offers excellent versatility whereby each level can be adapted to suit individual needs.

The Floating Seahorse Villas can also be customized to ensure the ultimate in privacy by fully enclosing the outdoor areas at sea level and on the upper deck. The Signature Edition of The Floating Seahorse Villas is designed especially for families with children and groups. The Floating Seahorse Villas is fully-customized and personalized to suit your individual style and taste. Inspired by the true meaning of a signature, each Floating Seahorse Villas will be bespoke and distinctive.
