Bloom Heights by Bloom Properties

Bloom Heights is a mixed-use urban development composed of residential and retail spaces located in the beautiful community of Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC). The project by Bloom Properties provides a total of 686 spacious residential units offering studios, one, two and three-bedroom apartments. The finishes of Bloom Heights will be inspired by modern and contemporary architecture,

with a smart design that allows the living space to be fully maximized. A host of high-end amenities can also be accessed within the opulent tower which include 2 swimming pools, a running track, multipurpose hall, retail shops, and a gym. The residences are specifically designed to provide affordable residences in a high-rise setting.
