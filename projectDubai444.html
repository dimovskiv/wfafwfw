Palm Villas by Ellington Properties

Ellington Palm Villas is a collection of exclusive and grand villas in the area of Palm Jumeirah. The villas offer eye-catching views of skyline city, as well as other dazzling leisure destinations in Dubai. Designed to focus on intelligent and rational use of space, Ellington Palm Villas truly exudes the very heart of residential living. Contemporary in shape and form, the residences color palette for the facade combines earthy desert shades of metal, glass & stonework.

The style & sophistication of Ellington Palm Villas will greet you upon entry with its thoughtfully-selected stone & wood flooring. The sanctuary of its pool & gardens also creates an environment for residents to get entertained, to relax and contemplate.
