Sweden Beach Palace by The Heart of Europe

Inspired by the structure of Swedish Viking vessels, Sweden Palaces exudes both stunning design and functional features. It is situated in front of a magnificent shoreline of The World Islands. Each roof of the seven-bedroom villa is similar to an upturned hull of a Viking ship, while its interior is relaxing to the senses with its unique, sophisticated design. Enjoy being so close to nature while being connected with modern amenities with Sweden Palaces.

Facilities in and around Sweden Beach Palace

  • Private landscaped gardens for privacy with over 67 species of plants, trees and shrubs
  • Floor to ceiling glass walls on every floor and bedroom with balcony access
  • Private beach access with full ownership rights
  • Coral populated sea from Kleindienst Marine Life Centre, with oysters for a diving and snorkeling experience
  • Access to events, restaurants and attractions at The Heart of Europe resorts and facilities
