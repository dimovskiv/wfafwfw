Premium Amenities

The Autograph Collection features luxury living spaces inspired with cosmopolitan flair and accents. he homes feature patterned marble floors, expansive floor-to-ceiling windows, a ceiling height of up to 4 meters, with a separate family lounge. The minimalist designs by The Autography Collection in The Residences JLT are seen in its soft, ambient light and subtle textures.

Easily access a wide range of amenities within the luxury building including an elegant lobby, a fully-equipped fitness center with steam and sauna facilities, a multipurpose room that is ideal for intimate gatherings and parties, a yoga room, swimming pool and a treatment room for a relaxing massage after a long day.

Convenient Location

The Residences JLT is situated in the sought-after community of Jumeirah Lakes Towers. The area is designed with nature-inspired landscapes, man-made lakes, and waterways. Residents can easily access retail outlets, dining areas and several leisure facilities in a vibrant community. The location is also seen as an emerging business area with various office buildings within the vicinity. The neighborhood of Jumeirah Lakes Towers is easily accessible through the Jumeirah Lakes Towers Metro Station.
