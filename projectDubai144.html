Convenient Location:

When choosing a dream house, one of the most important considerations for anybody is the convenience of location that place enjoys. Under this scenario, Tonino Lamborghini by Oriental Pearls enjoy a very feasible and nuclear location bearing high reach and accessibility. Tonino Lamborghini Residences are situated in the upbeat, central and vibrant area of Nad Al Sheba. As Tonino Lamborghini advertises and puts it: “Nestled between the royal palace grounds and the lavish private homes of the Dubai elites lies a pristine enclave in Nad Al Sheba”. This serene development has garnered wide global and local recognition due to its central location serving as the home of the Dubai World Cup boasting lush green lawns, breathtaking landscapes, and world-class shopping and dining.
