The Nook by Wasl Properties

The Nook, as its name implies, is a serene beautiful corner in the busy and commercial hub Sheikh Zayed Road by the much applauded and appreciated Wasl Properties in the region. The Nook is a uniquely structured and designed set of homes comprising of spacious and commodious studio, 1BR, 2BR & 3BR apartments. All the houses within The Nook are thematically designed and follow a specific set of coherently themed artworks, colors and interior settings. The apartments are all designed keeping comfort and a cosy lifestyle in mind.

Premium Amenities

The Nook by Wasl Properties offers a plethora of state-of-the-art amenities and facilities for the residents within its reach. The houses all feature dedicated swimming pools for men and women. Gymnasium facilities for men and women at all times within the residential building also provides as a major facility for residents as they do not have to take out time specially to travel all the way down to gyms and pay huge amounts of money. A gated and secured kids’ play area serves as a means of spending a good time together for parents with their kids. In addition to all these upbeat facilities, luscious green walking tracks, jogging tracks and park area are further means of relaxation for the residents after a long strenuous day at work.

Convenient Location

The Nook is located on the vibrant and commercial road of the region i.e. Sheikh Zayed Road that falls in close proximity to all the major road networks of the city and is accessible to all kinds of commotion points i.e. metro stations, cab stands and bus stations.
