The Residential Villas by Falconcity of Wonders

The Residential Villas of the Falconcity of Wonders are situated in the master development’s Falcon Wings. The villas guarantee a superb lifestyle as residents can choose from four distinguished home themes. The project offers 2, 3 and 4-bedroom townhouses, as well as 3, 4 and 5-bedroom semi-detached villas and 4 and 5-bedroom detached villas.
