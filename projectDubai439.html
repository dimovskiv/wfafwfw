Urbana II Townhomes by Emaar Properties

Urbana II by Emaar offers a new concept of tiered townhomes and standard townhouses. It is a mixed-use development in the first phase of Emaar South. Urbana II features residences which offer views of a stunning 18-hole championship golf course within the community. The cluster homes are ideally positioned at the crossroads of Dubai and Abu Dhabi, and just a few minutes away from the EXPO 2020 site. With its shaded walkways that link neighborhood pools and parks, anyone can engage the family in a healthy and active way of life community.

Enjoy recreation time with a round of golf, followed by a rejuvenating massage at the health club. Cap off a perfect day with a meal and refreshments surrounded by your family and friends at the clubhouse.

Unique Features

  • A 200-acre championship golf course
  • Reliable care with modern clinics and daycare centers equipped to meet the needs of the entire family
  • Two business hotels available within the community offer state-of-the-art facilities
  • Being within easy reach of Dubai’s City Centre and the main roadway to Abu Dhabi
  • Branded recreational facilities, convenient shopping, a resort hotel, and a well-appointed community center
