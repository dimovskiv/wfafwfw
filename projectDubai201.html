Lawnz by Danube Properties

Based in Dubai International City, Lawnz Apartments by Danube Group is quite obviously a country-themed residential complex comprising of studio, 1BR & 2 BR apartments. Based in the Warsan Community of Dubai International City, the development’s infrastructure has been built and designed in an uber-contemporary manner to be a fit with its surroundings. The apartments are all designed with immaculate intricacy and along the lines of a metropolis lifestyle approach that seconds the very idea of the community this residential complex is based in. Lawnz Apartments by Danube Homes, as the name implies, is a green development with big foliages hanging out of the balconies of the homes within and one of the main attractions to this development is that the luxury houses are available at extremely affordable rates and easy payment plans. The interiors of the apartments are master-planned to be spacious and designed in a way that makes them commodious for the residents to embellish according to their own tastes and preferences.

Lawnz by Danube – Open Space

Premium Amenities

Lawnz Apartments by Danube Group is a residential community well-equipped with very quintessential amenity required by the residents. The development has a well-furnished health centre providing gym, yoga, as well as exercising space to the residents. Not to miss, there is a dedicated play area within the development for kids with the provision of carefully planned play-areas for children. In addition, all the apartments have access to the spacious swimming pool and tennis court. Not only that, but the development has a face-on view of the breath-taking skyline of Dubai, and the luscious green parks in the vicinity.

Lawnz by Danube – Swimming Pool and Kid’s Pool

Convenient Location

Lawnz Apartments by Danube Group is based in Dubai International City and this very fact already makes it a very admirable, convenient and central location. The residential complex has access to all the travel touchpoints like metro links, cab stands, and bus stops. Based in Warsan Community in Dubai International City, the development has direct access to important as well as recreational places like Dubai International Airport, Dubai Design Centre, the Dragon Mart etc.
