Silver Springs Villas by Damac Properties

Silver Springs is a residential project by DAMAC Properties located in the world-class development of DAMAC Hills. Silver Springs features premium villas and townhouses ranging from three, four, five and six-bedroom units. Each home ensures that residents can enjoy maximized living spaces in a lush green neighborhood, with access to nearby communities such as Golf City, Layan Community and Arabian Ranches. The premier location is also accessible to Downtown Dubai if you want to enjoy world-class dining places and coffee shops while admiring the cityscape. Prices for its three-bedroom unit start at AED 3,193,000 (2,776 sq ft.).
