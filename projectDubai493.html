The 8 by IFA Hotels and Resorts

Inspired by a cool, chic Miami style living, The 8’s design is sophisticated and clean with a contemporary touch. Lightweight architecture and wide open spaces are utilized throughout the design, from the exterior focus on wide-open balconies to the airy and sleek interior adorned with modern finishes. The 8 by IFA Hotels and Resorts is located in one of the Palm Jumeirah’s most distinguished addresses.

Units are available in a variety of types covering two-bedroom and three-bedroom apartments, as well as signature townhouse and penthouse units. Each floor plan has been meticulously designed: from the private gardens, the entertainment decks and pools in the townhouses, to the private lifts of the duplex penthouses. Whether you prefer to spend your day lounging by the pool or snorkeling in the Gulf, The 8 by IFA Hotels and Resorts upscale facilities offer something special for everyone.
