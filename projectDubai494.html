MAG 5 Boulevard by MAG Property Development

MAG 5 Boulevard is a new residential development situated in the promising area of Dubai South. Spread over the expansive area of 800,000 square feet, the project is drafted with 13 ultra-modern, medium-rise buildings offering the best affordable housing in the city of Dubai. The residences are conceptualized to match urban living through a community where every inch is crafted with magnificent architecture. Comprised of retail, leisure, entertainment, and dining facilities, the project will offer 1,172 apartments through its residential development.

The proposed site is attached with one retail building to offer residents convenience at walking distance. This said retail building within the community will hold shops and service outlets to provide residents with more options for their commodity purchases.

Amenities

  • Balcony and parking spots for each unit
  • Swimming pool, jogging tracks, and gardens
  • Elegantly designed landscaping
  • Retail, dining and leisure outlets and activities
  • Located next to the ‘Green Belt’, the largest park in the UAE
