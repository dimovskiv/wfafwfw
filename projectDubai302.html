Nikki Beach Residences by Meraas Holding

Nikki Beach Residences is a premium residential project which will offer a total of 63 branded apartments which varies from 1, 2, 3 and 4-bedroom units. The branded residences will also include 6 townhouses and 1 penthouse. The homes will feature modern, minimalist and resort-inspired residences using the finest materials. The homes are also located in close proximity to the 450-meter shoreline stretch spread across the resort.

Premium Amenities

The homes in Nikki Beach Residences are designed to provide a glamorous lifestyle experience for its guests, with large comfortable rooms with modern and contemporary designs. The homes are equipped with cutting-edge technology and in-room entertainment ensuring the comfortable living for the family.

Residents of Nikki Beach Residences will have access to high-end amenities with the building and within the community. Access a state-of-the-art gym and multiple food and beverage outlets. The community will also feature 3 restaurants and an ultra-VIP lounge. Other features that the residents can visit within the community are the Nikki Spa by ESPA, Tone Fitness Center, and the brand’s famous beach Club. Nikki Beach Resort and Spa Dubai have been conceptualized in collaboration with DSA Architects International with interiors by Gatserella Design.

Convenient Location

Nikki Beach Residences is located in Pearl Jumeira, a reclaimed island in the Gulf that spans 8.3 million square feet with views of the Dubai Skyline. The community features 7 residential areas, a community town center with retail facilities, schools, mosques, beaches, waterfront hotels, and a unique two-kilometer promenade. The location promotes the joy of island living while staying connected to the city. Step out of Nikki Beach Residences and access a white and sandy beachfront within a secured and gated community.
