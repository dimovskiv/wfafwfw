Prime Views by Prescott Real Estate

Prime Views by Prescott is a modern-day contemporary residential venture of the developer in the vibrant neighborhood of Mohammed bin Rashid City in Meydan. A splendid amalgamation of comfortable living with an urban-ish feel to it, Prime Views offer 1BR & 2BR immaculately designed and structured apartments in the midst of the sports and cultural hub of Meydan. Prime Views entails a mid-rise building engulfing all these impeccably curated apartments just perfect for living.

With a profound significance given to a sustainable and healthy lifestyle, the residential development on all sides is surrounded by luscious green foliages. The location of this residential development is so nucleus that it is engulfed on one side by these natural hubs and on the other side contains a breath-taking view of the famous Dubai skyline. This is certainly a major grab for people looking to invest their money in the right place.

Premium Amenities

Prime Views by Prescott entails a plethora of the finest amenities to offer. From large spacious gardens, parks, walking and jogging tracks to swimming pools and health clubs in the vicinity, Prime Views has it all covered. The residential development of Prime Views is surrounded on all sides by the finest leisure spots, diners, restaurants, retail centers as well as shopping malls.

Convenient Location

Prime Views by Prescott is situated in a very accessible and central location at Al Meydan. It has a feasible access to some of the most significant commotion points in the city i.e. a 3 minute distance to Meydan One Mall, a 5 minute distance to Dubai Water Canal, a mere 7 minute distance to Downtown Dubai, Business Bay, and DIFC and a very close proximity to Dubai International Airport at a distance of just 15 minutes.
