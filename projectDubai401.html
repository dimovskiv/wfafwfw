Arabella 3 Villas and Townhouses by Dubai Properties

Arabella 3 is the third phase of development of Arabella project by Dubai Properties. The new phase was unveiled after the successful sales demand for the units in Arabella 1 and 2. The prime residences are located within the magnificent Mudon community, which is considered as one of the emerging neighborhoods in Dubai.

The opulent location provides residents with a healthy lifestyle with its cycling and walking tracks, as well as pristine gardens with abundance of natural light. The residences are also just a few minutes away from Mudon Central Park which exudes tranquility with its vast green spaces and tall trees, perfect for a relaxing family bonding. The residences also allow access to different amenities like a grocery store, children’s playground, restaurants, coffee shops, pharmacy, nursery and health club.

Accessibility

Mudon, the flagship of Dubailand, comes with the advantage of great location. It has direct access to Emirates Road and Al Qudra Road and is only 5 mins away from Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road.
