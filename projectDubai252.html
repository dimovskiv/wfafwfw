Parklane Residence by Dubai South Developer

Parklane Residence is a high-rise apartment project in the Parklane development in Dubai South featuring studio, 1, 2 and 3-bedroom apartments. The contemporary residences are situated near a clubhouse, retail outlets, and a lush green park. The homes boast of elegant living spaces with amazing views of the Dubai South (Dubai World Central) community, courtyard and the park.

Premium Amenities

Parklane Residence apartments provide its residents with a wealth of premium amenities accessible within the tower’s premises. Enjoy state-of-the-art facilities and amenities such as swimming pools and well-equipped gym for your health and fitness needs. Stroll around the Parklane community and enjoy nature in its green parks with a playground for children. Parklane Residence is situated in a secure and gated community with round-the-clock safety and community services.

Convenient Location

Parklane Residence apartments are located within the carefully-designed Parklane development in Dubai South. The area lives up to its branding as a “purpose-built community with a green heart”, by providing modern urban lifestyles within a natural environment. Parklane Residence is only a few minutes away from the EXPO 2020 site. The homes are also in close proximity to the Al Maktoum International Airport and other key entertainment and economic zones like Jebel Ali and Dubai Parks and Resorts. Easily accessible within the magnificent location are a sports center, community center, school, gas station, hospital, mosque, nursery, malls, and hotels.
