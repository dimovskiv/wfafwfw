J8 Apartments by Aqua Properties

J8 Apartments is a unique residential project offering the prestige and security of gated-community living within the Al Sufouh neighborhood. The project is composed of a ground floor plus eight storey low-rise residential structures, where residents can enjoy the pleasures of entering the home to a five-star lobby. Other premium amenities include a rooftop garden, a refreshing infinity pool, and other facilities such as a gymnasium, sauna, and steam room. J8 Apartments are also in close proximity to Sheikh Zayed Road which gives residents access to the entire city. With majestic views of Burj Al Arab and the Arabian Sea from the tower’s windows, the magnificent development creates an aura of relaxation and grandeur.
