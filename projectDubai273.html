Montrell by Azizi Developments

Montrell Apartments is a selection of apartments by Azizi Developments in Al Furjan, designed with elegant interiors, splendid views and smart designs that ensure a comfortable living ambiance. Premium Homes is being provided to you here. You can choose between a studio or a one-bedroom apartment. And, you can either make it your permanent home or your new investment.

Premium Amenities

Montrell By Azizi gives you furnished and serviced apartments. Now, these furnished apartments are built with sophistication and modernity, and it is where all the finest amenities and facilities are in. In turn, the condos have 24-hour reception and concierge service, valet parking facility, an open-air podium café, a state-of-the-art spa, a swimming pool and a play area for children. And lastly, there are also retail spaces that are and can easily be accessed through and within the building.

Vibrant Location

The vibrant location of Al Furjan is a magnificent neighborhood connected to major road networks including Sheikh Zayed Road and Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed. Easily travel to major destinations including Jumeirah Beach Residence, Downtown Dubai, Ibn Battuta Mall, Jumeirah Lakes Towers, Dubai Marina, and TECOM. Transportation stops are also within reach, including a metro station. Enjoy a variety of dining options including restaurants and cafes. In Al Furjan, you can unwind and relax with its outdoor pools, spa, and other luxurious meeting places.
