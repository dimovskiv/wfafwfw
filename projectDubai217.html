Elite Downtown Dubai by Triplanet Range Group

Elite Downtown is an amazing residential project by Triplanet Range Group situated in Downtown Dubai tastefully designed to suit the residents’ choices. The 24-storey luxury tower is Downtown Burj Khalifa District features one, two and three-bedroom fully-furnished apartments with wide balconies all showcasing Burj Khalifa and fountain views. The residences are priced affordably for its location and also come with a 3-years payment plan. The homes range from approximately 500 square feet to about 3000 square feet.

Elite Downtown – Burj Khalifa & Community Views

Premium Amenities

Elite Downtown features the best amenities at par with the standards of Downtown Dubai. Among its main features are a five-star premium swimming pool where you can take a dip after a long day. The apartment tower will also feature a fully-equipped gymnasium, sauna and prayer room. The premier apartments in Elite Downtown Dubai are all designed with wide open living spaces, exquisite bathrooms and spacious balconies.

Elite Downtown – Swimming Pool

Convenient Location

Elite Downtown is located in one of the most coveted addresses in the world, Downtown Dubai. The luxury master development, popularly known as a “city within a city”, features not only the best residential towers but also the finest shopping centers including Dubai Mall, entertainment venues, and leisure destinations. Despite the upscale lifestyle in this modern neighborhood, the residences in Elite Downtown are offered at much affordable prices compared to other similar residential properties in the location. You can now live and enjoy the upscale Downtown lifestyle without compromising your budget with Elite Downtown apartments.
