Venetian Tower- Canal Residence West by Dubai Sports City

Canal Residence West – Venetian Tower by Dubai Sports City (DSC) developer is a 20-storey residential tower located in the Dubai Sports City development. The ready tower is one of the five major apartment buildings, with the Mediterranean and European Towers also offering ready-to-move apartments. Canal Residence West – Venetian Tower features a total of 274 units ranging from 1, 2 and 3-bedroom apartments. The apartment tower was launched in 2008 and was completed in June 2015.

Premium Amenities

The Canal Residence West – Venetian Tower features stylish living spaces and facilities inspired by Venetian architecture. The homes are spacious and designed with excellent layouts, including an en-suite bathroom, semi-closed kitchen and a wide living room connected to a balcony (for selected units). The residences are also provided with basement parking spaces and 24-hour security and maintenance. Access shared facilities within the building including a swimming pool, barbeque area, kids’ play area, gym, and sauna. Canal Residence West – Venetian Tower also features a private club that provides a state-of-the-art health club, beauty salons, therapeutic treatments, reading and music room, games room, as well as a business center. The residences also provide easy access to supermarkets, coffee shops, and restaurants.

Convenient Location

The Dubai Sports City development is the premier location of Canal Residence West – Venetian Tower. The lush community which is known as an international sporting venue for golf and cricket tournaments is also a strategic location for several residences like mid-rise apartments and villas. With international schools, hotels, leisure areas, and lifestyle and country clubs within the area, residents are provided with every they need all within their reach. Canal Residence West – Venetian Tower is also in close proximity to a sports-themed retail mall with a leasing capacity of 1.2 million square feet, which will feature different shops, retail plazas, and boutiques.
