Vincitore Boulevard by Vincitore Real Estate Development LLC

Vincitore Boulevard is a mixed-use residential and retail development located in close proximity to the stunning Miracle Garden in the Arjan community. The project will be comprised of six buildings that will cover over 173,000 square feet of land a total area of 300,000 square feet. The mid-rise building has classic architectural designs and will be consist of retail and leisure activities on the ground floor. The top two floors, meanwhile, will be for residential and serviced apartments. Vincitore Boulevard also allows residences to gain access to modern amenities like a swimming pool, gymnasium, sauna bath, kids play lounge and a tennis court.

Vincitore Boulevard offers stylish studio apartments and one-bedroom apartments with the starting price of AED 555,000*.
