Anantara Residences by Seven Tides International

Anantara Residences is a luxurious residential project by Seven Tides International offering furnished apartments and penthouses in the magnificent Palm Jumeirah development. The ready-to-move residences feature 442 apartments, composed of one-bedroom and two-bedroom residences.  Built with finest quality furnishings and one-of-a-kind interior designs, these homes are among the top choices in the community. Anantara Residences apartments feature breathtaking views of the Gulf, Atlantis, Burj Al Arab, and the Palm.

Premium Amenities

Anantara Residences offer living spaces equipped with elegantly-fitted wooden floors, walk-in closets, and built-in wardrobes. These residences are furnished with top-of-the-line furniture and appliances for its bathroom and kitchen. The floor-to-ceiling windows of Anantara Residence allow an abundance of natural light. Its living room is powered by an Opus smart home system. Residents can also access a shared gymnasium, swimming pool, spa and sports areas for their health and leisure activities. Young kids are also provided with a kid’s play area that is both safe and fun.

Anantara Residences offer services that are at par with 5-star hotels, the same level of service offered to the guests of its resort. Included in its exclusive à la carte services are room service, babysitting, concierge services, housekeeping, and valet parking – all ensuring convenience and escape from the stress of everyday life.

Stunning Location

Stroll around the Palm Jumeirah beach community and find the finest range of restaurants and cafes that will surely satisfy your cravings. Anantara The Palm Dubai is also a luxury beach resort at the outer crescent of Palm Jumeirah. Residents and visitors alike can savor the beauty of its lush gardens and crystal clear lagoons which are lined with palm trees. The designs of the community are inspired by rich cultural traditions and historic heritage which blends perfectly with the natural beauty of the destination.
