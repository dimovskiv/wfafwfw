Villa Amalfi by Meraas Holding

Villa Amalfi at Jumeirah Bay is Meraas Holding’s most recent addition to its existing chain of the most astounding & revolutionary residential projects. Amalgamating luxurious living with a comfortable lifestyle bearing access to all the defining necessities of life with 3BR, 4BR & 5BR Villas, Villa Amalfi at Jumeirah Bay is surely one of the most anticipated ventures in the region. This immaculate development comprises of luxurious villas intricately designed and adorned and serving as the canvass of some of the state-best interior designers. All the villas own their separate spacious lawns and courtyards providing a beautiful view of the stunning Jumeirah Bay in the surroundings and a direct access to the beach.

Premium Amenities

The residential community of Villa Amalfi at Jumeirah Bay possesses a plethora of some of the finest amenities. Premium facilities like large swimming pools, a dedicated gym, a spacious beach and marina access as well as proximity to the best set of hotels, retail outlets, diners, and restaurants are some of the key location pluses that residents can enjoy at Jumeirah Bay. In addition to all these facilities, the residential development entails tennis courts, basketball court and dedicated lounges to help residents feel at home.

Convenient Location

Jumeirah Bay is a highly accessible and central location from a residential perspective. Villa Amalfi, therefore, will enjoy an extremely key location with some major destinations of the city within its close reach. Jumeirah Beach Road lies at just a 2 minute distance from these luxury villas. In addition, Sheikh Zayed Road, Dubai International Airport, and Burj Al Arab are also some locations lying in close proximity to Villa Amalfi at Jumeirah Bay.
