Bella Rose by Deyaar Properties

An epitome of modern and urban lifestyle, Bella Rose as a feather is one of the latest additions to the already over cluttered hat of achievements. This residential venture, as its name implies, is all things beautiful, tranquilizing, stunning and serene. Comprising of spacious thematic studio and 1BR apartments, Bella Rose by Deyaar is the ultimate haven and habitat for a facilitated living. All these studio and 1BR apartments are fully furnished and follow an eclectic theme that further adds to their elegance and the whole feel of the house. All the houses are built and constructed keeping residents’ ease and convenience in mind. A project of Deyaar, the residential development strictly cohorts to sustainable living and has been designed to follow the same patterns too.

Bella Rose by Deyaar – Bedroom

Premium Amenities

Bella Rose markets this residential development as the mediator between fitness and fun because it provides a plethora of options for both. Be it its state-of-the-art gymnasium, the spacious tennis court, mini golf court, a basketball half court, or the swimming pool aka infinity pool, Deyaar has got you covered with all the superior lifestyle amenities. In addition to all these, the insertion of large open spaces, bbq areas, comfort lounges in the vicinity add further sentiment to the whole place.

Bella Rose by Deyaar – Amenities

Convenient Location

Bella Rose by Deyaar is situated in Dubai Science Park. It is due to this central location, it enjoys close proximity to some of the major locations in the city. At a bare 1 minute drive to City Center Al Barsha, 4 minute drive to the astounding Dubai Miracle Garden, 12 minute drive to the grand Mall of the Emirates, and 22 minutes of drive to Dubai International Airport, this residential address qualifies perfectly to serve as a home to many families, singles and couples alike.
