Fifty One by Deyaar Properties

Fifty-One is a magnificent commercial & office building located in the heart of Business Bay. This stunning tower is the perfect place for your next office space, is located in a dynamic and fast-developing community where the majority of huge local and international businesses are located.

The low-rise commercial tower by Dubai-based developer Deyaar is composed of 21 floors with stylish and spacious office spaces with stunning views of the Dubai Historical Creek. Each unit is designed with a combination of functionality, elegance, and convenience which perfectly understands investor expectations and customer profiles. The location of these residences is also in close proximity to the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building the world; The Dubai Mall and a number of world-class hotels.
