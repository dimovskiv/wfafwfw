Sherena Residence by ASAS Holding

Sherena Residence is a premium luxury residential apartment located in the heart of Majan. The project has been developed to cater to the growing demand for luxury apartments in Dubai. “Sherena” means “pearl oyster” and it was called so as a project is similar to an oyster that houses pure pearls. With a total of 3 buildings, Sherena Residence offers its investors spacious and luxurious studios, 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, and 3-bedroom apartments.

Every apartment at Sherena Residence promotes wide spaces and natural light, allowing residents to enjoy an unparalleled lifestyle with the opulent living spaces. This exclusive apartment complex provides its residents with a variety of world-class amenities such as a large indoor swimming pool, fully- equipped gym, steam and sauna facility and play areas for children and a lot more.
