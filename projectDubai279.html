Al Haseen Residences by City Properties

Al Haseen Residences is a premier residential project located in Dubai Industrial Park, one of Dubai’s largest industrial hubs. The address is one of the most coveted locations as every home provides comfort and security.

Premium Amenities

The residential project will welcome you into its magnificent lobby marked with chic and noble designs. Savor a magnificent ambience in every apartment with its carefully curated bedroom. With fixtures and fittings of superior quality, residents are ensured that each home is furnished with top-of-the-line amenities.

Among the top-shelf amenities by Al Haseen Residences are its swimming pool for recreation and relaxation and its two contemporary gyms completely equipped with facilities that will help you maintain your healthy and active lifestyle.

Stunning Location

Located in the heart of Dubai Industrial Park, the residences are adjacent to Dubai South and minutes away from the Al Maktoum International Airport. EXPO 2020 venue and Dubai Parks and Resorts. The premier homes are also located in close proximity to Jebel Ali Port. The community which is free from congested traffic is also connected to Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Road and Emirates Road.

The location of Al Haseen Residences also provides convenient access to global transportation points, either by road, air or sea. Being a thriving business hub, the residents can also enjoy an easy commute to work every day without losing spending too much time on the road.
