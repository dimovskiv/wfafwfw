Al Habtoor City by Al Habtoor Group

Al Habtoor City is a magnificent residential project along a waterside location on Sheikh Zayed Road. The Al Habtoor City project features two luxury residential towers with luxury penthouses, three of which considered as ultra VIP – as it will feature 360-degree panoramic views of the Dubai Skyline. The multi-use development features top-rated hotel by Starwood all in one plot – St. Regis, Westin, W hotel. The two towers of Al Habtoor City stand 75 storeys and 52 storeys consist of 1,460 apartments. The project also has a 7-storey luxury podium with a basement level and five floors dedicated to parking spaces and retail. Al Habtoor City will be offering residences from 5 to 7-bedroom apartments with canal views from every apartment.

Premium Amenities

Al Habtoor City features a wealth of premium amenities within the towers and even outside the community. The project features a state-of-the-art tennis academy with 3 outdoor tennis courts and one indoor air-conditioned court and clubhouse. The towers feature 22 high-end and gourmet restaurants which include a 24-hour restaurant called Level Seven. The Al Habtoor City project also features the largest leisure deck in the world which includes a swimming pool, kids pool, fitness center, and play areas. The European-style boulevard would also feature around 50 retail and alfresco cafe outlets such as Bentley Cafe.

Convenient Location

Al Habtoor City is located in a city within a city along Sheikh Zayed Road. The 10 million square feet development is the first freehold project in the area. The location is just 5 minutes away from Downtown Dubai and the DIFC and has access to a water taxi stop and helipad. The area is also the home to the best theatrical shows in the world: Franco Dragone La Perie (a Las Vegas-style show) by the same creator a Cirque Du Soleil. All of the 3 hotels within the area come under Starwood group allowing owners and residents discount privileges on F&B, spas and preferential room rates.
