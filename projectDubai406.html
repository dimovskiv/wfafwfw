The Atria by Deyaar Properties

The Atria are high-end serviced apartment towers by Dubai-based developer Deyaar located right at the center of Dubai’s commercial and trading hub, Business Bay. These premier residences provide absolute satisfaction to any homeowner with its cozy and spacious bedrooms and living spaces, as well as stylish bathrooms, kitchens and dining areas.

The contemporary serviced apartments are designed by dynamic design house Yoo Studio which was founded by Philippe Starck and John Hitchcox. This sought-after address assures that each of their residential units is the hallmark of a good life.
