Micasa Avenue by Diamond Real Estate Developer

Micasa Avenue is a stunning residential project by Diamond Real Estate Developers which features unique apartments located in the heart of Al Furjan. The low-rise building features apartments with high-quality finishes available in 1 and 2-bedroom units.  The project got its name from the Spanish phrase “Micasa” which means “My Home”.

Premium Amenities

The stunning residences feature a wealth of amenities such as an elegant lobby and a communal garden. For people with an active lifestyle, Micasa Avenue has its own well-equipped gymnasium. Adults and kids alike easily enjoy leisure time with its own swimming pool and a children’s play area. Sleep soundly at night without fear as the building as a 24-hour security system.

Vibrant Location

The magnificent community of Al Furjan is home to Micasa Avenue apartments. The vibrant neighborhood is complete with a variety of neighborhood facilities and amenities. Micasa Avenue is strategically located in a community linked to major road networks like Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Road and Sheikh Zayed Road.  Residents can easily travel to major destinations in Dubai such as the IBN Battuta Mall, Jumeirah Lakes Towers, Dubai Marina, TECOM, Jumeirah Beach Residence and Downtown Dubai. Transportation stops are also accessible within the area, including a metro station.
