Bluewaters Residences by Meraas Holding

Bluewaters Residences by Meraas is a range of vibrant apartments, penthouses and podium townhouses located off the coast of Jumeirah Beach Residence. The homes are carefully designed to cater most especially to families, with the range of destinations surrounding the unrivalled development. The area is also set to become one of the world’s largest tourism hotspots. The mid-rise towers of Bluewaters offer one, two, three and four-bedroom glass-front apartments provided with parking spaces. With state-of-the-art amenities, the homes offer a premium lifestyle for the whole family.

Bluewaters Residences

Premium Amenities

Bluewaters Residences are composed of 10 apartment buildings, 4 penthouses and 17 podium townhouses that promise a unique island experience. Totaling to 698 one, two, three and four-bedroom glass-fronted apartments providing one or two parking spaces. The whole family can easily access state-of-the-art gymnasiums, swimming pools, landscaped gardens, basketball courts and children’s play areas. Step out and freely avail all the island facilities and enjoy walking around its landscaped courtyards, pedestrian areas and shaded walkways. The four 5-bedroom penthouses of Bluewaters Residences showcase 360-degree views of the Gulf, Marina, Ain Dubai observation wheel and the Dubai skyline. Access retail areas, restaurants, cafes and entertainment locations within the ground and first floor of the premier tower.

Bluewaters Residences – Interior

Convenient Location

Directly linked to Dubai’s main artery, Sheikh Zayed Road, residents of Bluewaters apartments can easily explore different destinations in the emirate. Bluewaters will also be operating the Group Rapid Transit service which offers passengers travel through a driverless vehicle system. High-end restaurants and bistros will also be surrounding the area, as well as parking spaces for shoppers and visitors. The residences are just a few meters away from Ain Dubai, the world’s largest observation wheel. The location of Jumeirah Beach Residence is composed of the finest restaurants, shopping strip and several high-end hotels within the vicinity.
