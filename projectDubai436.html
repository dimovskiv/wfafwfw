One Palm by Omniyat

Located at the track of Palm Jumeirah, One Palm is a yet another luxury residential project by Omniyat. Built, designed, and crafted by the renowned award-winning New York firm SOMA Architects. One Palm has become an epitomic architectural statement, located at the beginning of the world’s largest man-made island, the Palm Jumeirah. All of the residences’ interiors at One Palm by Omniyat are structured and adorned by a popular Japanese interior design firm Super Potato and London-based interior design firm Elicyon, both of which have harnessed its imagination through their different approaches of utilizing different materials. The homes inside this spectacular residential development are available in 3-bedroom simplex, 4-bedroom double-height living area, and a 5-bedroom duplex penthouse. One Palm is managed by Dorchester Collection, a five-star hotel operator known to have managed top-tier luxury hotels in Europe and the USA.

The One Palm show apartments for the three-unit types of One Palm are available for viewing.

Premium Amenities

One Palm Apartments features a wide selection of premium amenities and a la carte services which make your everyday living an absolute breeze! Apart from classic services such as maintenance, doorman and valet parking, One Palm also provides its residents with special services such as housekeeping, in-house dining, catering, unique dining events, and butler service-just as in Burj Al Arab. All of One Palm’s services are designed to provide smooth VIP assistance, to its residents. These services include a selection of dining options, events, venues, tickets, and all the best social services in the city. Come and enjoy the breeze from your new home.

Convenient Location

One Palm is perfectly nestled in the stunning community of Palm Jumeirah, home of pristine beaches and modern islands surrounded by Gulf waters. The world-class destination which was first launched in 2006 has been known for its high-end retail, vibrant and amazing nightlife and one-of-a-kind Dubai’s beachfront living experience. One Palm Apartments is set at the forefront of the finest hotels in UAE including Burj Al Arab, Jumeirah Zabeel Saray, Kempinski Hotel, Atlantis in The Palm, Kempinski Hotel and Waldorf Astoria Dubai. From your home, you can admire the stunning city skyline of Dubai with the Gulf as the backdrop.
