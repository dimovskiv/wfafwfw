Al Badia Hillside by Al Futtaim Real Estate Group

Al Badia Hillside by Majid Al Futtaim are traditional Arabic-inspired homes nestled in an elevated hillside in Dubai Festival City. Reminiscent of the old-fashioned hillside village along the Mediterranean coast, these homes make you travel back in time while letting you enjoy the best of modern comforts.

The sophisticated residences feature a range of studios, 1, 2 and 3-bedroom apartments as well as 3-bedroom duplexes. Residents can enjoy stunning views of the Dubai Creek, Dubai Skyline and the lush greenery of the Al Badia Golf Course.

The magnificent homes of Al Badia Hillside also provide premium amenities such as basement parking, enclosed courtyards, and open terraces.
