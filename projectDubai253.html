Parklane Townhouses by Dubai South Developer

Parklane Townhouses is a residential project in the Parklane development in Dubai South featuring 2, 3 and 4-bedroom townhouses. The 406 stacked townhouses by the Dubai South developer offer the finest living spaces in close proximity to a clubhouse, park and several retail outlets. The townhouses are composed of different clusters with simplex and duplex types to cater to the varying needs and preferences.

Premium Amenities

Parklane Townhouses provide elegant townhomes with the dedicated balcony that lets you enjoy the community’s stunning views and is built with separate garden space. Each living space is designed high-quality materials and spacious layouts that further enhance the living experience. Accessible within the area is a state-of-the-art gym and recreational area perfect for those who aim to ensure a healthy lifestyle. The residential area also provides large swimming pools for a refreshing dip to unwind and relax. Enjoy nature in its lush green parks equipped with a playground for kids, all within a gated community with 24-hour security and community services.

Convenient Location

Parklane Townhouses are located in the Parklane development in Dubai South. The development brands itself as a “purpose-built community with a green heart”, an innovative urban living concept within lush green spaces. The homes are just 5 minutes away from the EXPO 2020 site, in close proximity to the Al Maktoum International Airport and other major entertainment and economic zones such as Jebel Ali and Dubai Parks and Resorts. The premier location also provides easy access to a sports center, community center, school, gas station, hospital, mosque, nursery, malls, and hotels.
