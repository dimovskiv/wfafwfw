History of developments of Abu Dhabi

The capital city of the United Arab Emirates is also the most populous Emirate in the country. Given an area of 67,340 sq km, it is also the largest of all the emirates. With several rulers residing within the region, the Emirate of Abu Dhabi also serves as a home to the ruling families of United Arab Emirates and is undoubtedly the most poshly built and designed Emirate of all the seven.

Why should you live in Abu Dhabi?

With the quintessential Arab land vibe to it, Abu Dhabi is much more than sand, seas and beaches. It does not only offer the top-notch leisure it is famous for world-wide, but also a very stable and balanced economic side of life to the residents. The average income per person is fairly high within the region as all the central commercial and industrial offices are located within the Emirate. Thus, people deciding to reside within the region can be rest assured that they will enjoy an economically stable life and would be able to afford the daily life expenditures of the place. The hub of opportunities comes up with a more diverse array of futuristic opportunities ranging from science to arts to commerce to performing arts and design etc, thus, giving a chance to a happy-life to people coming from all kinds of different career, cultural and educational backgrounds. Not just that, the Emirate is also home to the best schools, colleges, and universities of the country. Hence, Abu Dhabi is a perfect fit to build a home in. With several career opportunities flooding in, Abu Dhabi can perfectly serve as a major getaway for the residents too with so many islands, resorts, and recreational places within the region. In terms of transportation, The Department of Transport (DoT) provides commotion facilities within the whole region through buses, trains, ferries etc making life all the more easier for people residing within the Emirate.

Major Recreational Atrractions Of The Emirate:

Abu Dhabi is not only an ideal fit for residential reasons but also for a plethora of recreational landmarks it homes. Major destinations like Ferrari World, Yas Water World and Yas Island, the Sheik Zayed Grand Mosque, and Emirates Park Zoo etc. make Abu Dhabi all the more interesting and intriguing as a place to live in and take your children too.
