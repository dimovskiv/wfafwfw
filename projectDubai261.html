LIV Residence Apartments by LIV Developers

LIV Residence is a collection of luxury apartments in Dubai Marina designed to provide a unique opportunity for investors. The homes are scheduled for completion in 2019, just in time before the much-anticipated EXPO 2020. The G+27 tower designed by NAGA features the finest living spaces and a host of exclusive facilities. LIV Residence in Dubai Marina features studio, 1, 2 and 3-bedroom apartments.

Premium Amenities

The residences are thoughtfully designed with floor-to-ceiling windows, allowing natural sunlight to permeate the rooms, and showcasing stunning views. The floor plan and design maximize the use of space, allowing its residents to enjoy each part of the home. Furnished with European-styled kitchen and upgraded appliances to ensure convenience without compromising style and elegance.

Once you step into the premises. LIV Residence welcomes you with an awe-inspiring lobby, high ceilings and ambient lighting that are meticulously chosen for the sophisticated resident. The interiors are created by the world-renowned MWM Studios, reflecting expertise which was honed for years in the 5-star hospitality industry. The homes also feature spacious terraces that show full views of the marina and the sea.

Stunning Location

Live at the forefront of luxury and elegance in Dubai Marina! LIV Residence allows you to enjoy the trendy beaches and stunning Marina, along with its premium line of shopping avenues, dining areas and entertainment destinations at the heart of Dubai. The homes are not just connected to the beach, but also to transportation stops all in one coveted address. Living in Dubai Marina also links you to best city landmarks and vistas including The Atlantis, Palm Jumeirah, Jumeirah Beach Residence and whole lot more.
