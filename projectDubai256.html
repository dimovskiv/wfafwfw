Zeest Apartments by MS Homes Developers

MS Zeest Apartments by MS Homes Developers is a selection of the premier luxury apartments located in International City Phase 3, right at the very heart of Dubai. The outstanding residences feature studio, one and two bedroom apartments with the finest interiors and designs. The homes offer a good investment opportunity being in an affordable location.

Premium Amenities

MS Zeest Apartments offers unique living spaces crafted with choice materials for an optimum comfortable lifestyle. Featuring movable wooden louvers, residents now enjoy the option of having a wider private space, which also gives the project a constantly changing facade. The amenities and facilities of MS Zeest Apartments are of high quality and standards to make sure that every resident gets the best value for their money. Unwind at its beautiful swimming pool or relax by its poolside gazeebos. Maintain your fitness goals by hitting the gym for some workout sessions and enjoy other health club amenities within the building. Access retail spaces and landscape areas right inside the premier tower, allowing residents of all ages to enjoy a comfortable living.

Convenient Location

The prime location of MS Zeest Apartments in International City places it at the melting pot of various nationalities and cultures which makes Dubai a modern and cosmopolitan area. The master development is designed with a vision to provide budget-friendly yet elegant residence to over 60,000 people. International City has been one of the best places to own home not only because of its affordability factor but also because it keeps you connected to the best destinations in Dubai. Easily access major road networks like the Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Road; main destinations like Burj Khalifa, Dubai Airport, Academic City, Silicon Oasis; and shopping centers like Mirdif City Center and Dragon Mart.
