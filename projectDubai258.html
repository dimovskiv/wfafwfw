District One Residences by District One

District One Residences (G+4) is a range of contemporary apartments located in the beautiful community of Mohammed bin Rashid (MBR) City District. The residences offer the perfect lagoon view where you can enjoy a cup of coffee during the day or admire stunning city views during the night. The residences offer various designs including modern and contemporary style apartments. The individual apartments are already sold out, but its full building for sale (with one and two-bedroom apartments) is still available.

Premium Amenities

District One Residences (G+4) offers the finest amenities within a secured and gated community. With 600 acres of lush greenery and easy access to a 7-kilometer lagoon with 14-kilometer shoreline, you can be as close to nature as you can get. Head down for a quick swim at glistening crystal lagoon or simply enjoy the sunset. Stroll around its boardwalks, or enjoy nature in its local and community parks for a quick break from your busy schedule. The residences are also surrounded by a commercial center, local retail and grocery for your basic necessities all within your reach. There are also educational institutions like a nursery or kindergarten school, clinic and health centers and within the area.

Stunning Community

District One Residences (G+4) is located in the Mohammed bin Rashid (MBR) City, a mega-development popularly known as a “city within city”. The premier location will be home to a varied selection of dining destinations, high-end shops and retails outlets, healthcare centers, and landscaped parks. The master development is linked to major road networks as it is nestled between Sheikh Zayed Road, Emirates Road and Al Khail Road. The master development is also located minutes away from Downtown Dubai, which is a modern metropolis of Dubai’s luxury landmarks.
