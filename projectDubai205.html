Azure Residences by Nakheel Properties

As the name rightfully describes, Azure Residences is a proper epitome of Nirvana! Of all the things Dubai is famous about, the beach-life in Dubai has patented itself as one of the most sought after in the global picture. Facing a beachfront and based on the eastern shoreline of Palm Jumeirah, Azure Residences offer 1B, 2BR residential apartments with intricate interiors and finest urban furnishing. Offering a breathtaking view of the pure waters of the Arabian Gulf, Azure Residences is a ten-storey structure consisting of a total of 170 1BR and 2BR apartments. The development has been conceptualized and designed keeping in mind its appeal to people looking to reside in, people who would like good fruitful investments as well as tourists who are on a getaway and deserve all-things-beautiful.
