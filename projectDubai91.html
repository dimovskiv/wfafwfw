Wilton Park Residences by Ellington Properties

Wilton Park Residences by Ellington Properties is a mixed-use luxury tower in Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum  City. The residences are perfectly nestled at the center of Dubai’s shopping and entertainment destinations and are also connected to major Dubai highways and public shipping centers.

Aside from providing studio, 1 and 2-bedroom apartments, the premier tower also offers retail shops, restaurants and lounge areas. Gain access to a wide range of amenities including a fully-equipped gym, steam, and sauna, outdoor swimming pool, barbeque patio exclusively for its residents.
