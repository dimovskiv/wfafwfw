Germany Island Villas by The Heart of Europe

Fully-equipped with state-of-the-art green energy, Germany Island Villas offers an eco-friendly way to relax and enjoy leisure time. Fifteen beautiful beach villas and 17 lagoon villas will be within an exclusive neighborhood near a stunning beachfront view. Germany Island Villas infuses minimalist and new age design concepts from modern Germany architecture and Bauhaus luxury lifestyle. All of the homes will be intricately designed using excellent quality materials and it will be area maximized to provide spacious living spaces.

Germany Island Villas offers its hotels to investors at competitive prices. In addition, the developers of the villas also offer the option of “owning your own beach”.
