City Walk by Meraas Holding

City Walk by Meraas is an upscale residential development by Meraas with a concept of a “city within a city”. The residences are designed within a community surrounded by shopping, entertainment, hospitality, wellness and living options in one integrated community. City Walk is consist of a total of 34 five and six storey low-rise apartment building which is seen to accommodate over 200 residents. The project has now released 3 residential buildings namely City Walk Residential Buildings 5, 13B and 16.

City Walk Residential Building

Citywalk Residential Building 5 will have a built-up area of 26,158 square meter in a ground floor plus six floors (G+6) structure. The 66 apartments are divided into 14 one-bedroom, 35 two-bedroom, 15 three-bedroom and two four-bedroom units with one or two parking spaces.

Premium Amenities

Residents of any of the City Walk Residential Buildings will have full access to a ground floor landscaped public courtyard, public terraces, a multi-purpose area, gym & changing facilities, roof deck, swimming pool and kids pool, and kids’ play area. The low-rise pavilion-style buildings also has access to a range of entertainment concepts, dining options and even retail areas. Residents can also access a well-secured boulevard that showcases exclusive fashion brands, boutique car showrooms, arts and craft galleries, designer home decor boutiques, art and music studios and food & beverage choices within a sophisticated, outdoor setting.

City Walk Outdoor Facilities

Convenient Location

The City Walk Development where the City Walk Residential Buildings are located allows residents to have leisure and entertainment choice all within reach. Residents can access various attractions such as Hub Zero, the first immersive entertainment park; The Green Planet, the region’s first bio-dome; Mattel Play! Town, a themed education-and-play attraction for kidsl and Reel Cinemas, a 10-screen luxury multiplex cinema. The area will have its own healthcare and wellness component care of Valiant Clinic, an out-patient facility providing diagnostic and wellness services that focus on preventive care. There will also be an urban hotel within the vicinity and public facilities such as mosque and underground parking space. The City Walk development is also located in close proximity to Sheikh Zayed Road, Downtown Dubai and the Jumeirah Beach Road.
