Bellevue Towers by Dubai Properties

Bellevue Towers is a collection of elegant residential apartments that boast modern and sophisticated designs with breathtaking views. The project was launched by Dubai Properties Group along with the tract of Downtown Dubai.

Bellevue Towers is composed of two residential towers, the east and west buildings, which comprises a total of 300 apartments across the 23 floors, ranging from one, two and three bedrooms units, penthouses and lofts. Residents will enjoy a high-end recreation and fitness facilities with its gym and infinity pool. With its unique and majestic residences, Bellevue Towers has become one of the most sought-after properties in Dubai.

Key Features

  • 1, 2, 3 bedroom apartments, penthouses, and lofts
  • Apartments range from 545 sq.ft to 3,000 sq.ft (80 sq.m to 280 sq.m).
  • 15m high ceiling in the living/dining room.
  • Views of <a href="https://dxboffplan.com/properties/burj-khalifa-residence-downtown-dubai/">Burj Khalifa</a> and Downtown Dubai.
  • Unique façade lighting.
  • Spacious and modern lobby.
  • Gym facilities with a view.
  • Located in the Downtown Dubai district with views of <a href="https://dxboffplan.com/development/business-bay/">Business Bay</a> and <a href="https://dxboffplan.com/development/dubai-water-canal/">Dubai Water Canal</a>.
  • Community Spaces
  • Walking distance to Dubai mall &amp; Marasi promenade on the Dubai Canal.
  • Luxury property with a high-quality finish.
  • Dedicated retail shops and restaurants, a wide variety of amenities including an infinity pool, gymnasium, and play areas.
