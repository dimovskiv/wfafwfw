The Nest by Al Barari Developers

The Nest at Al Barari is a range of elegant and stylish homes in the nature-inspired Al Barari development. This selection of 55 villas is ideal for large families as it offers 4 luxury bedrooms that differ in design and lifestyle themes. The homes allow an abundance of natural light to come in and showcase views of the majestic Al Barari gardens. These premier residences are available in two distinct designs allowing flexibility for every resident.

Premium Amenities

The Nest at Al Barari is designed with unparalleled amenities and lush surroundings in the area. The residences are designed with functional and high-quality materials for its living room, dining area, and contemporary kitchen. With luxury finishes including high-end porcelain tiles and white quartz stone on the kitchen countertops, Its kitchen cabinets are designed by Colombini Casa while its appliances are designed by Zanussi.

In addition to its elegant interiors, The Nest at Al Barari features a private swimming pool surrounded by the beautifully landscaped garden. Each of the villas provides a dedicated accommodation for your maid and driver.Â  Enjoy the best amenities including a driver’s room, barbeque area, and two car parking spaces.

Convenient Location

The Nest is nestled in the amazing community of Al Barari designed to be a botanical haven, with eco-friendly properties and surroundings. A huge part of the community is designed with green spaces, landscaped lakes and freshwater streams. This astonishing surroundings of the 18.42 million-square-foot development will be featuring over 200 villas. The area will also feature a gourmet restaurant The Farm, a state-of-the-art health club Body Language, and destination wellness and spa called Heart & Soul. All the residential areas are covered with thick forests and set within an exuberant community. Residents of The Nest in Al Barari can have access to its children’s play areas within the community.
