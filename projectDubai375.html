Rawda Apartments by NSHAMA

Rawda Apartments by NSHAMA is a four-building apartment complex located in the vibrant development of Town Square Dubai. The opulent residences boast of picturesque views of the Town Square park, the courtyard, and the main Town Square Boulevard. Residents can enjoy high-class amenities within the community like swimming pools, children’s pools, play areas, private gardens, gym and concierge services. A wealth of destinations can also be accessed within the neighborhood, including residential facilities, landscaped public spaces, local retail outlets, and upscale restaurants.

Rawda Apartments ensure the best connectivity within the emirate, as the residences are located between major road networks Al Qudra Road and Emirates Road. Town Square’s location is also situated very close to public transportation, coffee shops, as well as huge shopping malls. The development provides a collection of exclusive facilities like an outdoor cinema (Reel Cinemas) and a hotel spa (Vida Hotel Spa). Hospitals, schools, and mosques also surround the beautiful community to cater to the residents’ needs.
