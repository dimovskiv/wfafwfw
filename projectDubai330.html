Amaranta Townhouses 2 by Dubai Properties

After the successful sell out of the previous phase of Amaranta Townhouses, Dubai Properties is launching its new phase for the premier residential project: Amaranta Townhouses 2.

Property Features

Amaranta Townhouses Phase 2 is a selection of elegant residences located in the vibrant Villanova neighborhood. Inspired by Mediterranean architecture, these residences provide a luxurious yet comfortable ambience which perfectly suits its serene surroundings.

Premium Amenities

Enjoy an active lifestyle right at your doorstep, as Amaranta Townhouses 2 provide wide open spaces and landscaped walking trails surrounded by a wealth of palm trees. Enjoy premium amenities in the location of this newly launched Amaranta phase including a well-equipped gym, sports courts for different types of sports, swimming pool and a mosque within the community.

Vibrant Location

The vibrant community of Villanova is strategically positioned and connected to different activity areas in Dubailand. Linked to a major road network called Emirates Road Highway, residents can easily have access to the city’s gateway.  The residences of Amaranta 2 may be connected to the modern metropolis, but its community remains tucked away in a peaceful location with lost green spaces and trees.

Key Features

  • Part of 23.174 million sq. ft. lively and vibrant gated community with lush greenery and a developed retail area.
  • Direct access to Emirates road and Academic City-road, situated near to the intersection of Al Ain road and Shaikh Mohammed Bin Zayed road.
  • Diverse, life-enhancing amenities such as schools, nursery, kids play area, BBQ areas, sports facilities, cinema, jogging track, shopping and F&amp;B outlets.
  • Villanova is gracefully strung together by lush green Linear Parks dedicated for pedestrians.
  • Close proximity to Dubai’s major landmarks.
  • Offers spacious Townhouses of 2, 3 &amp; 4 bedrooms ranging from 1549 sq. ft. to 2213 sq. ft.
