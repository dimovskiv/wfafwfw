Vida Residences Sky Collection by Emaar Properties

Soon after causing a stir in the urban market with Vida Hotel in Downtown Dubai, Emaar Properties took a leap of faith and landed right in the apex of the residential scenario. Vida Residences Sky Collection is an immaculate residential tower located on the globally famous Mohammed Bin Rashid Boulevard comprising of a whopping yet not so surprising (given its right opposite to the miracle i.e. Burj Khalifa), 57 storeys spread across 3 podium levels. There could be, in no way, a closer and more scenically apt place to invest it than this of Vida Residences Sky Collection as it very much lies in the lap of roughly what UAE on the whole is known for globally! As the manifesto behind the creation very aptly explains: ‘In short, it is perfect’, there could genuinely be no fancy way to explain something that is quite self-explanatory due to its very existence and its location itself. However, having said that, the residential tower comprises of 3BR, 4BR and 5BR apartments with state-of-the-art infrastructure inspired by ‘New York Art Deco’ architecture of the 20’s.

Premium Amenities

Not so surprisingly, given Vida Residences Sky Collections’ prime location in Downtown Dubai, the residential development is linked to Vida Downtown via an air-conditioned glass bridge connecting all three levels of the podium residences to the hotel. Hence, this mere bridge opens up the door to all world-class amenities Vida Downtown popularly contains and has to offer. e.g. Luxury diners, hotels, restaurants, leisure centers, retail options etc.

Convenient Location

Located adjacent to the center-piece of gulf’s attraction i.e. Burj Khalifa and lying right there in the heart of Dubai, Vida Residences Sky Collection is a residential community located in an ideal location in terms of its proximity to transportation channels, commercial touch points, as well as major business districts of the Emirate.
