Golf Promenade by Damac Properties

Golf Promenade is a range of apartments set perfectly between the Championship-standard golf course of the Trump International Golf Club Dubai and The Drive AKOYA. The residences, inspired by the Beverly Hills lifestyle, is nestled in a world-class 18-hole golf course along with two and a half kilometers of luxury shopping, dining and entertainment right at your doorstep. The buildings of Golf Promenade themselves are marvels of modern architecture that blend seamlessly with their glamorous surroundings. The two stunning buildings with floor to ceiling windows are linked by a spacious terrace where the luxurious swimming pool is situated. These fantastic living spaces are spread across 142 units of studios to four bedrooms and 8 townhouses. In Golf Promenade, you will enjoy uninterrupted golf course views all through the day at the comfort of your home.
