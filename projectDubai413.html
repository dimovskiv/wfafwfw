XXII Carat Club Villa by Forum Group

XXII Carat Club Villas are luxury residences located in Palm Jumeirah developed by Russian-backed property developer Forum Group. The villas exhibit sheer opulence by offering magnificent beachfront views of the stunning Palm Jumeirah community.

22 Carat Club Villas – Private Beach Access

Premium Amenities

These homes are one-of-a-kind, with its spacious living spaces and stylish European-inspired designs and finishes. 22 Carat Club Villas also allow access to high-end amenities which includes a 720-feet private beach, private and members’ swimming pools with poolside cabanas, and a club house.

22 Carat Club Villas – Swimming Pool

Each of these luxury villas in Palm Jumeirah is designed with classic Tuscan accents and Mediterranean-inspired architecture that perfectly complements its lush landscapes. The luxury villas offer up to seven-bedroom arrangements, ensuring utmost standards for every facility.

XXII Carat Villas provide professional-grade kitchens and custom bathroom cabinetry complete with lightings designed by master French glass cutters, generous floor ceiling heights and expensive deck floor terraces. the These residences are purposefully designed to give a hotel-inspired lifestyle, with five-star service offered 24/7 by the on-site concierge staff.

Mansion Districts

The villas in 22 Carat Palm Jumeirah have been established and structured in three types namely Emerald, Ruby, and Sapphire. Emerald is composed of 8 villas with exceptional views from the ground floor and private gardens laden with exotic flowers and lush greenery with views of the Dubai Skyline. Ruby includes 8 villas with private elevators that allows direct access to the basement garage and has a wide open rooftop terrace area ideal for family gatherings. Sapphire boasts of 6 villas with a waterfront setting and direct private beach access and offers the most spacious floor plan of all the villa types.
