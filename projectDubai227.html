Seven Hotel Apartments by Seven Tides International

Seven Hotel Apartments by Seven Tides International is a range of luxury hotel apartments located in the trunk of the breathtaking Palm Jumeirah. The iconic building is designed to be a eye-catching beauty in the midst of the different establishments in the trunk., providing an all-in-one lifestyle of luxury, convenience and harmony.

Premium Amenities

The Seven Hotel Apartments will feature luxury fittings and finishes as well as floor-to-ceiling windows that showcase stunning views of the Gulf, Marina skyline and the Dubai Eye. Each bedroom has soft drawer walk-in wardrobes with en-suite bathrooms with rain showers. The kitchens are fully-equipped with white goods and state-of-the-art appliances. The hotel apartments also feature separate controls on the air-conditioning system, allowing the owner a full-control of the units’ ambience.  The hotel apartments are available in studio, one, two and three-bedroom units.

Beautiful Location

Seven Hotel Apartments surely is your next dream home being located in the stunning, great community of Palm Jumeirah. The luxury building will be home to a number of retail outlets on its ground floor including a grocery convenience store, dry clean shop, pharmacy and other necessary amenities. Seven Residences will also be nestled between 5 star hotels, Fairmont & Dukes Dubai. The premier development has a direct beach access, as well as a beautiful infinity pool. There are also two food and beverage outlets and a fully air-conditioned walkway which connects residents directly to the Nakheel Mall.
