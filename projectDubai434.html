The Pad Apartments by Omniyat

The Pad is one of the most luxurious and stunning furnished apartments developed by leading Dubai real estate developer Omniyat.  The Pad is located at the waterfront of Dubai Creek, in the tract of Business Bay, just off the commercial corridor of Sheikh Zayed Road. The tower accommodates of 253 stylish apartments which are offered in various arrangements such as studios, one and two bedrooms apartments, and the loft.

Amenities and Facilities

  • Landscaped gardens
  • gymnasium
  • Swimming pool
  • Entrance lobby and corridors: Stone floors, stone/ painted gypsum/ feature walls, painted gypsum ceilings
  • Landscaping: Paving and planted areas
  • Located in the heart of the Burj Khalifa District
  • Fantastic views of the Burj Khalifa, Mohammed Bin Rashid City, and the Creek
  • The building includes duplex lofts and garden apartments
  • Convenient access to the Sheikh Zayed Road and Al Khail Road
  • Few minutes’ drives from the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC), Trade Centre, Dubai Mall, and Dubai Design District
