Serenia Residences by Palma Holding

Serenia Residences is an exclusive collection of bespoke apartments by Palma Holding located in Palm Jumeirah. These homes are designed with absolute architectural excellence.

Its fusion of nature elements to its modern homes brings to life the vision to develop Dubai’s most exclusive beachfront residences. The residences are available in 1, 2, 3 and 4-bedroom luxury apartments and presidential penthouses with private roof gardens. Serenia Apartments boast of 360-degree views of the Dubai Marina, The Palm and Burj Al Arab.

Premium Amenities

Serenia Residences feature residences that you will surely love, blending contemporary architecture with the laidback beachfront lifestyle. The homes feature floor to ceiling windows and wide balconies to admire full views of the sea.

Serenia also features gardens with water elements within the community, accentuated by barbecue areas and fire pits that can be enjoyed by its residents. Its premier gymnasium is designed with a modern all-glass wall overlooking the beach, while its Olympic-size swimming pool is just outside the gym premises. Sports and fitness enthusiasts can enjoy a game in its private tennis courts or hit the road running in its custom-built running track that surrounds the complex.

Convenient Location

Serenia Residences is located in The Palm Jumeirah development, located on a unique beachfront plot in on its crescent. Every home will not only have amazing views but also easy access to key Dubai destinations including the Nakheel Mall, Waldorf Astoria, Anantara Dubai The Palm Resort and other areas.

Fine dining restaurants, landmark hotels, entertainment places, retail shops, and world-class spas will also be just within your reach.
