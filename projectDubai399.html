The Court Tower Apartments and Offices by Tanmiyat Global Real Estate

The Court Tower is a luxury apartment and office tower located at the premier community of the Business Bay. The high-rise building offers the first apartments to be delivered at the Dubai Water Canal, offering a full panoramic view of the magnificent water channel. The luxurious apartments are located on the topmost floors (23rd to 30th floor) of the tower and are offered in a studio and 1-bedroom arrangements

Aside from residences, the mixed-use tower also offers high-quality and spacious offices and retail spaces. The building has parking spaces in its 5 basement floors, retail areas located on the ground floor and podium, and offices located at the 1st to the 22nd floor. The location of Court Tower is the most popular area for offices rent-optimal investment with a guaranteed high return.

Features

  • Near main entrance of business bay from Sheikh Zayed Road
  • Near Business Bay Metro Station
  • Near Dubai Financial market which is moving to business bay
  • Unobstructed views of Dubai Water Canal
  • Retails are located in the ground floor and podium
  • Can accommodate 6 offices each floor (from 1st floor to 22 floor)

  • 960 parking spaces on the 5 basements
  • Each office have provision for toilet and pantry
  • Common toilet is available on each floor
