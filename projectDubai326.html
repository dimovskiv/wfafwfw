Jumeirah Luxury by Luxury Living Investments

Jumeirah Luxury by Luxury Living Investments is a collection of 291 contemporary villas (and townhouses) located in the community of Jumeirah Golf Estates. Comprised of 16 communities, these homes are set across 1,119 hectares of green landscapes, an unrivalled location with world-class amenities. The gated community perfectly secures the residences within a sociable community atmosphere with interesting leisure facilities.

The luxury homes are best connected via Mohammed Bin Zayed Road, which is close to Jumeirah Golf Estates’ main entrance. Jumeirah Luxury villas are also just 15 minutes away from Palm Jumeirah, Dubai Marina. Al Maktoum International Airport and the site of EXPO 2020. Surrounding the area are schools, shopping malls, hospitals, supermarkets, restaurants, and entertainment centers.
