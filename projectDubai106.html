Dubailand Residence Complex (Plot) by Dubai Holding

Dubailand Residence Complex (DLRC), is a unique freehold project complementing Dubailand’s entertainment and leisure destination. The development offers the highest standards of modern living, featuring mosque’s, schools, and healthcare amenities. Strategically located between the Emirates and Al Ain highways, DLRC offers convenient access to the Trade Center, Academic City, Meydan Racecourse, and Dubai International Airport.

DLRC is a master-planned community that is mainly created for residential use. Having a spread of 14.7 million square feet, DLRC boasts of large spaces and an open area which accommodates every need of its occupants.  Neighboring the villa project, DLRC is conveniently located near the highway and has close proximity to the institutions of Academic City.
