Imperial Avenue by SP International Property Developers

Imperial Avenue by Shapoorji Pallonji is a collection of luxury residences located in Downtown Dubai. The towers’ fluid design complement the magnificent metropolis where the iconic Burj Khalifa is located. The 45-story tower offers 424 elegant apartments with 5 levels of parking and 3 levels of wonderfully landscaped podiums with best facilities. The residences in Imperial Avenue are available in 1, 2 3 and 4-bedroom apartments and 5-bedroom penthouses. There are also 3, 4 and 5-bedroom villas with private pools at the podium level.

Premium Amenities

Imperial Avenue residences are designed with stunning interiors and beautiful mix of colors, materials, and textures. Dominated by a color theme of autumn gold and rust plus swirling patterns, residents will feel utmost elegance and sophistication within the home. The exquisite homes also feature designer bathrooms, modular kitchens and private decks that coincide with a chic-themed unit.Â  its living rooms and bedrooms are also decorated with classy yet pleasing finishes that allow you to relax after a long day. The premier luxury apartments are also equipped with unique water features, stylish lobbies, and high-speed elevators.

Upon entering Imperial Avenue’s premises, you will be greeted by 10,000 square feet lobby that is warm yet discreet. There are free-flowing podiums at the 6th and the ninth levels which feature kids play area and a family pool. There is also a rooftop infinity pool overlooking the city skyline. The fully-equipped gym allows keeping your fitness goals, while the indoor recreation area keeps you entertained over a game of squash.

The homes’ other unique features include a private AV room, solar panels installed in the roof for water heating, greywater treatment plants (sustainable water source), drinking water purifier and an LPG connection.

Stunning Location

Being located in Downtown Dubai which is called the “Centre of Now”, Imperial Avenue is at the heart of the finest leisure, shopping, dining and entertainment destinations in Dubai. All within a five-block radius, you can easily explore the latest fashion boutiques, luxury dining places, and attractive boulevards. The residences of Imperial Avenue are just a few minutes away from Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, The Dubai Fountain, Downtown Boulevard, Business Bay, 5-star hotels and the Airport.
