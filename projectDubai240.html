Naseem Townhouses by NSHAMA

Naseem Townhouses by NSHAMA is a sought-after residential project in Town Square Dubai, and it boasts of a perfect community that is providing comfortable living and pleasant surroundings. In the same way, these premier living spaces are available in 3 and 4-bedroom townhouses, as well as several layouts to choose from. And, each home in Naseem Townhouses are tailor-fitted and carefully planned to cater to your needs.

Premium Townhouses

So, the elegant and stylish homes of Naseem Townhouses provides the best amenities that go with your lifestyle. Accordingly, the Town Square Dubai neighborhood is complete with everything you need, from outdoor facilities and activity areas including biking trails, jogging tracks, skate parks, and Town Square Park as well as community gardens. Then, enjoy a wide array of restaurants, cafes, and retail outlets all within the area. Sports, fitness, and leisure are also within reach, and since Town Square Dubai provides a football field, tennis court, outdoor gyms, and a swimming pool. In addition, the location also provides another unique leisure experience with its outdoor Reel Cinemas besides its regular indoor movie houses. So the area also has ample parking spaces for its residents and guests. And, with its fire-fighting and alarm system, you are therefore assured of your safety in the neighborhood especially even in times of emergency.

Convenient Location

Naseem Townhouses is strategically located in Town Square Dubai, and it is a sought-after location that is nearly adjacent to the entrance of the development. Moreover, it is respectively situated at the intersection of Al Qudra street and the Emirates Road. Furthermore, the area is only a few minutes’ drive to the Mall of The Emirates, and also in close proximity to the Al Maktoum International Airport and The Dubai Mall. Then, the development will feature a huge central public space, and it would be the size of 16 football fields, as well as feature 350 retail outlets. Finally, the Naseem Townhouses will be surrounded by areas of a secluded parkland that allows you to enjoy nature within a modern community.
