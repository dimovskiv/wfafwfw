Samana Greens by Samana Developers

Samana Greens by Samana Developers is a range of high-end apartments located in Arjan in Dubailand. The premier residences featuring studio, one and two-bedroom apartments, with each home exclusively tailored to provide unparalleled quality that can stand the test of time. Samana Greens are designed with luxurious architecture and interior design to ensure that high-quality standards of living. These residences are set to be completed in April 2020, which is six months before the much-awaited EXPO 2020.

Samana Greens – Luxury Apartments

Premium Amenities

Samana Greens features a great selection of upscale amenities that cater to the residents’ interests including fitness, sports, fun, relaxation, entertainment and other facilities that suit all ages. Secure your fitness goals with its state-of-the-art gymnasium, and relax and get rejuvenated in its sauna place. The project also features secure kids play area, themed retail area, basement parking and lots of green spaces that creates a refreshing ambience all around.

Samana Greens – Swimming Pool

Convenient Location

Samana Greens is located in the strategic development of Arjan, situated next to the popular and inspirational attraction, Dubai Miracle Garden. The stunning garden which features over 45 million flowers has been one of the best tourist destinations in Dubai. As a whole, Arjan is also foreseen to be an international destination with various cultural attractions and recreational facilities. The homes are accessible through two major expressways – Al Khail Road and Mohammed bin Zayed Road. Samana Greens are also surrounded by major Dubai communities including Arabian Ranches, Sports City, Jumeirah Village Circle and Dubai Hills. The outstanding project is also close to the Al Maktoum International Airport, the world’s largest green-field airport project which has the capacity to handle 160 million passengers annually. The worth of the property is also of high value being close to the venue of the EXPO 2020 site in Dubai South.
