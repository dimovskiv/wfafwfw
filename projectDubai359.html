Remraam Apartments by Dubai Properties

Remraam Apartments by Dubai Properties is a collection of attractive yet affordable homes located in the Remraam Development in the heart of Dubailand. These residences are designed to ensure comfort and serenity, while maintaining luxury and quality standards that you desire in a home. Remraam Apartments Offers 1-bedroom, 2-bedrooms & 2+ maid’s room apartments units at very reasonable prices.

Residents of Remraam are sure to enjoy the neighborhood’s extensive communal facilities which include pools of different sizes, sports areas, and kids play area among others; which area set across astounding landscaped gardens. Families can also enjoy picnics or barbecue time with dedicated areas perfect for get-togethers.

Being strategically located in one of Dubai’s best-connected communities, Remraam Apartments can be easily accessed through the Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road, Emirates Road and the Al Maktoum International Airport at Dubai World Central.
